TANK 1/2 LEVEL CONTROL SHUTOFF VALVE SW.

Three position spring loaded away from PRECHECK, toggle switch.

CLOSE

  • Closes related Refuel/Defuel/Transfer shutoff valve to stop fuel flow

PRECHECK

  • Simulates full fuel signal to the High Level Control Unit (HLCU) stopping refueling of related tank

OPEN

  • Opens, related Refuel/Defuel/Transfer shutoff valve for refueling or defueling
