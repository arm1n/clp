REFUEL/DEFUEL SELECTOR SWITCH

OFF Position

  • Power removed from the refuel/defuel control panel

PRESELECT REFUEL or DEFUEL Position

  • Refuel/Defuel operation stops automatically , when the preselected levels are reached

REFUEL or DEFUEL Position

  • Manual Refueling or Fefueling continues until the selected switch is set to OFF, the HLCU detects a full tank, or the level control shutoff valve switches are placed in the CLOSE position
