MFD Fuel Page.

FUEL CONTROL TRANSFER panel and the Fuel Page of the Multi Function Display (MFD).

The MFD Display shows the Fuel system data that follows:

  • Left/Right Fuel Tank Quantity
  • Fuel Temperature in Left Tank
  • Left/Right Fuel Transfer Valve Position
  • Fuel Transfer Switch Indication
  • Pressure State in the Left/Right Auxiliary Fuel Pumps
  • Total Fuel Quantity in Tanks
