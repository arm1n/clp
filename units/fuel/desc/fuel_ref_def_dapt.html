Refuel/Defuel Adapter

The refuel/defuel adapter gives single point pressure refuel/defuel access for the aircraft.

The refuel/defuel adapter is installed in the right engine nacelle behind the access door of the refuel/defuel panel.

The adapter has a metal body that has a spring-loaded poppet valve which closes after the fuel nozzle is removed.

A circular cap protects the poppet valve from damage and contamination.
