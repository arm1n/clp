ENGINE DISPLAY (Fuel Idication)

The Engine Display (ED) shows the Fuel system data that follows:

  • Left/Right Fuel Flow Digital Value
  • BALANCE Command Message (Fuel Limitation)
  • Left/Right Fuel Tank Quantity Digital Readout
  • Left/Right Fuel Inlet Temperature Digital Value
