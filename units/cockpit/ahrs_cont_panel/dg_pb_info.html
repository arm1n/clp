DG ANNUNCIATOR SWITCH (momentary action)

PUSH - key bar segment (green).

  • manually selects the DG Mode for display on the EHSI (selected after the SLAVE annunciator comes on)
  • the EHSI shows a DG heading source annunciation, the HDG FAIL annunciation and the slaving error indication goes out of view upon DG mode selection.

PUSH - key bar segment (blank), return to SLAVED mode.
