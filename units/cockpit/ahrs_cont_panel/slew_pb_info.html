SLEW PUSHBUTTON SWITCHES (momentary action)

PUSH - corrects the rotating heading dial for gyro drift.

  • the rotating heading dial turns in a clockwise direction while the + DG SLEW switch is pushed and it moves in a counter-clockwise when the ° DG SLEW switch is pushed.
  • if one slew switch or the other is initially pushed and held, the rotating heading dial turns at the low speed rate of 1°/s and if held for more than 3 s, it will then turn at the high rate of 10°/s.
