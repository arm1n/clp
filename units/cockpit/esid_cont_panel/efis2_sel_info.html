EFIS ADC SOURCE REVERSION SELECTOR (three position, rotary action)

  • ESCP provides the crew with the ability to control the ADC source reversion on EFIS

NORM - each side receives their own air data source:

  • ADC 1 for pilot side
  • ADC 2 for copilot side

Position 1, 2 - selection from the NORM position either to side1 or to side 2, indicates crossside air data source of attitude and heading is displayed.

  • when both sides are selected to the same air data source, ADC1 or ADC2 annunciation is displayed in yellow on each PFD
