WX / TERR PUSH-BUTTON (momentary action)

  • shows or turns off the display of the radar or Enhanced GPWS (EGPWS) symbology on the MFD NAV page, if ARC is selected and Weather / Radar and / or EGPWS are active.

PUSH 1 - removes the weather radar and EGPWS images (but WX / TERR operating mode is still displayed)

PUSH 2 - displays EGPWS terrain information.

PUSH 3 - displays weather radar information (default selection).
