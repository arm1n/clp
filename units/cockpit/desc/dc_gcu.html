DC GENERATOR CONTROL UNITS

Each DC GCU automatically controls the operation of its DC starter/generator by monitoring output voltage, current, and speed.
After an engine start, the DC GCU controls its generator output and controls the operation of its bus contactor K1 or K2.

The DC GCU regulates the DC starter/generator's output and gives the protective functions that follow:

Parameter Value Over voltage 32.5 ±0.5 VDC Diff Feeder Fault Detection 75 A ±25A Bus Fault Detection 700 A Parallel Load Sharing 10% rated current
