DC CONTROL PANEL

The DC control panel gives manual control of the main 28 Vdc generation system. It has toggle switches to control the systems that follow.

  • Batteries
  • DC starter/generators
  • External DC power
  • Main feeder bus tie
  • Bus fault reset
