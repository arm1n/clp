HYD/FLAP OVERRIDE CONTROL PANEL

The GPWS LANDING FLAP selector switch on the hydraulic control panel is set to one of three landing flap positions for the insufficient terrain clearance mode as follows:
  • 10 degrees
  • 15 degrees
  • 35 degrees

A related indication on the hydraulic control panel comes on to show the landing flap position selection. The aircraft flaps select lever is set at the same GPWS LANDING FLAP selector switch selection.

Aural Warn Mute Switch

An aural warning mute system has been provided to temporarily inhibit the Traffic Alert & Collision Avoidance System (TCAS}, Enhanced Ground Proximity Warning System (EGPWS) and Landing Gear Warning System (LGWS) warnings during aerial fire fighting.
Pressing the AURAL WARN switch causes the MUTE annunciation to illuminate (Amber) and inhibits all audible TCAS, EGPWS and LGWS warnings for a period of 2 minutes. Visual TCAS, EGPWS and LGWS warnings remain active regardless of AURAL WARN mute switch position.
