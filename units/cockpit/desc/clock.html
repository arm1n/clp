Electronic Clock

Each clock operates independently. The No. 1 Clock is interfaced directly with the Cockpit Voice Recorder and both clocks are interfaced with the Flight Data Recorder (FDR) through the Flight Data Processing System (FDPS).
The FDR normally records time from the No. 1 Clock but will switch to No. 2 Clock if the No. 1 Clock fails. Real time is recorded on both the Cockpit Voice Recorder (CVR) and Flight Data Recorder (FDR) to establish synchronization between the two recording systems.
