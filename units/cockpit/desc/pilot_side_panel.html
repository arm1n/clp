Pilot’s Side Console Panel

CIR BKR LIGHT SWITCH (two position)

  • CIR BKR LIGHT - pilot's circuit breaker lights comes on
  • OFF - pilot's CB light off

W/S WIPER ICE DETECT PUSHBUTTON (momentary action)

  • PUSH - windshield wiper ice detection light, above glareshield, shines on the pilot’s windshield wiper spigot

PILOTS FLT PNL KNOB (rotary action)

  • OFF - pilot's side console, ICP1, and Standby Instruments lights off
  • ROTATE - changes pilot's side console, ICP1 and Standby Instruments lights intensity
  • BRT - pilot's side panel console, ICP1 and Standby Instruments lights at maximum brightness
