AIR CONDITIONING CONTROL PANEL

BLEED 1 AND 2 SWITCHES (two position) Starts bleed airflow from the No. 1 and/or No. 2 engine to the air conditioning packs OFF − stops bleed airflow from the selected engine by closing the above mentioned valves

  • BLEED 1 AND 2 SWITCHES (two position)
      • Starts bleed airflow from the No. 1 and/or No. 2 engine to the air conditioning packs
      OFF − stops bleed airflow from the selected engine by closing the above mentioned valves

  • BLEED CONTROL SELECTOR (three position, rotary action)
      • MIN − allows Environmental Control System (ECS) controller to modulate the amount of bleed air from both engines at a minimum level.
      NORM, MAX − allows ECS controller to modulate the amount of bleed air from both engines at increased levels.

  • PACKS CONTROL SWITCHES (three position)
      • OFF − shuts related Air Cycle Machine (ACM) off
      MAN − selects analog channel of ECU to supply temperature control
      AUTO − ECU digital channel in control temperature control
