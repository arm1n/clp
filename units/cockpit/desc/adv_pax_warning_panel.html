will come on if the cabin attendant's EMERGENCY LIGHTS switch is set to the ON position
FASTEN BELTS SWITCH (two position)
FASTEN BELTS - fasten seat belts passenger signs come on
  • low tone sounds over the PA speakers
  • RETURN TO SEAT sign in the lavatory comes on

NO SMOKING SWITCH (two position)
NO SMOKING - no smoking passenger signs come on
  • low tone sounds over the PA speakers
  • no smoking signs also come on when landing gear selector is set to the DN position OFF
  • no smoking passenger signs goes off if the gear selector is not set to the DN position

EMER LIGHTS SWITCH (three position, lever locked)
ON Position
  • emergency lights come on if emergency battery pack are charged
  • lights powered from the emergency battery packs
  • aeroplane battery power not required
ARM Position
  • emergency lights armed
  • emergency lights will automatically come on if the L MAIN 28 VDC bus fails or is selected off (removed)
OFF Position
  • emergency lights go off if the flight attendant's EMERGENCY LIGHTS switch is in the NORM position
