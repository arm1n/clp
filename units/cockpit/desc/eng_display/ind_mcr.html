Engine Rating Mode Indications

ENGINE RATING MODE ANNUNCIATION (green)

Indicates selected engine rating mode

  • Maximum Climb Power (MCL) SHP 4058
  • Reduced Power Take Off (RDC TOP) SHP 3947
  • Maximum take-off power (MTOP) SHP 5071
  • Normal Take Off Power (NTOP) SHP 4580
  • RDC TOP will be displayed when in NTOP and reduced take-off power is selected
  • with RDC TOP switch on ENGINE Control panel
  • when data is not valid, 4 white dashes are displayed
