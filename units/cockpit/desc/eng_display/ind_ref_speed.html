INCR REF SPEED MESSAGE

When the INCR speeds selector is turned INCR, the Stall Protection System (SPS) changes its calculations because of the reduced performance limits of the aeroplane. The Engine Display (ED) of the Electronic Instrument System (EIS) shows an INCR REF SPEEDS message.

When the propeller anti-ice switch is turned ON, the Stall Protection System (SPS) changes its calculations because of the reduced performance limits of the aircraft.
