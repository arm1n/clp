STATIC TEMPERATURE INDICATION

The static temperature probe measures the surrounding aircraft Indi- cated Outside Air Temperature (IOAT). It is a flush-attached electrical resistance transmitter with temperature resistance properties.

The accuracy of the resistance over the –50 to +50 C range is ± 1 ohm. This resistance error is equivalent to a temperature accuracy of 0.52 C within this range.

The static temperature probe is attached flush to an access panel in the wing to fuselage fairing below the left wing root with four mounting screws.

NOTE: If Pilot reports Prop de-ice caution light cones on without selecting prop de ice, a possible cause is the dual sat probe.

Please check indication of 1 and 2 temperature indications on Pilots eng display and Copilots eng display for mismatch, a discrepancy of 8°C can cause the problem.
