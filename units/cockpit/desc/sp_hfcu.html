HF Communication CONTROL UNIT

HF Communications: The HF communication system transmits and receives high frequency communication signals in the 2.0 to 29.9999 MHz frequency radio spectrum with 100 Hz channel spacing. The system is tuned by a separate control unit and interfaces to the existing aircraft Audio Integration System (AIS) for audio monitoring and transmission control.

The HF communication system transmits and receives through a single HF shunt antenna that forms part of the vertical stabilizer leading edge.
