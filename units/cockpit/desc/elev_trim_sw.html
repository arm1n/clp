Handwheel Elevator-Trim Switch

A pitch trim split switch is located in the center of the outboard handgrip of each control handwheel. The pitch trim switches are split in two. Both halves of the switches must be activated for the pitch trim command to proceed.

The switches give manual control of the elevator pitch trim. They are thumb−operated switches, which are spring−loaded to the center−off position. When the switches are pushed forward, a nose-down trim is commanded and when the switches are pulled aft, a nose−up trim is commanded.
