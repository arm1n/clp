Autopilot Disconnect Warning Light

The A/P DISENG warning lights shows an AP disengage condition. The AP disengage warning lights are attached to the glareshield panel in front of the pilot and copilot in the flight compartment.


The A/P DISENG light on the left side

The A/P DISENG light on the right side
