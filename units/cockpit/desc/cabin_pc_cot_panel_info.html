Pressurization Control Panel


To control the pressurization system in auto, manual and dump.

  • 1. OUTFLOW VALVE Selection Switch (three position)
      • DUMP -releases pressure by fully opening the outflowvalve
      MAN -turns off the cabin pressure controller-enables manual control of the outflow valve and the cabin differential pressure using the MAN DIFF switch.
      AUTO - cabin altitude is controlled by the CPC using the outflow valve which modulates to maintain a pre-programmed cabin altitude schedule.

  • 2. FWD Outflow Valve Rotary knob (rotary action)
      • CLSD - increases the cabin altitude by closing the forward safety valve (forward safety valve set to closed)
      OPN - decreases the cabin altitude by opening the forward safety valve (forward safety valve set to open)
      Usually set fully counterclockwise (CLSD) when in AUTO mode.

  • 3. MAN DIFF Switch (three position, spring loaded to center)
      • NCR - decreases differential when AUTO/MAN/DUMP switch is in the MAN position
      DECR - increases differential when AUTO/MAN/DUMP switch is in the MAN position.

  • 4. LDG ALT Rotary Switch (rotary action)
      • Selects the field elevation for takeoff and landing as shown on the LDG ALT indicator.

  • 5. LDG ALT Indicator
      • Displays the landing altitude selected with the LDG ALT rotary knob.
