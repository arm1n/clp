Forward Safety Valve Selector

Opening the Forward Safety Outflow Valve provides smoke evacuation or ram air flow.

The forward safety valve selector is located on the copilot side console and completely opens the forward safety valve to dump the cabin pressure. The selector is a shut-off valve. NORMAL or OPEN positions can be selected with the rotary switch.

  • 1 Switch guard
      • - Lift to access forward safety valve selector.

  • 2. Forward safety valve selector (two position)
      • - NORMAL - forward safety valve fully closed
      - OPEN - opens the forward safety valve fully for emergency operations.
