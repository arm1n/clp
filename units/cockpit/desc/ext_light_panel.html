Landing and Taxi Exterior Lights Panel

APPROACH LIGHT SWITCH (lights located outboard of flare lights and angled inward)

  • APPROACH - left and right approach lights come on
  • OFF - approach lights off

FLARE LIGHT SWITCH (ights located just outboard of nacelles and angled downward)

  • FLARE - left and right flare lights come on
  • OFF - flare lights off

TAXI LIGHT SWITCH (ights located on the nosewheel)

  • TAXI - taxi light comes on if the nosewheel is in the down position on the ground or air-borne
  • OFF - taxi light off
