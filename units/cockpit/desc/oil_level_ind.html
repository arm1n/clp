Remote Oil Level Indication System (Optional)

The remote oil level indication system is installed on the Dash 8−400 as an option under CR812CH00006 (written as “system” in this description procedure). The system is non−essential for the operation of the aircraft.

The system gives indication that there is approximately 10 flying hours or less of engine oil (based on normal oil consumption).

The Aircraft Maintenance Manual (AMM) still requires the oil level check using the sight glass and low oil level indicator.
