AIR DATA COMPUTER

The two ADC are installed in the flight compartment on the aft bulkhead.The ADC measures static pressure (ps) and pitot pressure (pt) supplied from the primary pressure probes and compensates these pressures for probe position errors. The ADC also measures the temperature probe resistance to calculate Outside Air Temperature (OAT).With this data and and the barometric setting data supplied by the Index Control Panel (ICP), the ADC does the air data calculations.

From these primary parameters and with the acquired barometric setting information transmitted by the Index Control Panel (ICP), the ADC does the air data calculations.


The ADC is a Line Replaceable Unit (LRU) that has the components that follow:
  • Mechanical case that secures the pressure ports
  • Electrical board with two silicon pressure sensors P90, a CPU, analog and discrete inputs/outputs, ARINC 429 interface, power supply and electrical interface with a connector and EMC protection
