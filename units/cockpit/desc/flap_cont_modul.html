Flap Selector Lever/Quadrant

The flap selector lever and quadrant are located in the flight compartment on the right side of the centre console. The flap selector lever moves in a detented cam from 0 degrees (full forward) through detents at 5, 10, 15 to a 35 degree setting

Flap Lever Transducer The flap lever transducer is a single section, five-wire type Rotary Variable Differential Transformer (RVDT). The RVDT is mechanically operated by the flap selector lever and is electrically supplied by the FCU. The RVDT is designed to sense the angular position of the flap selector lever.

NOTE: Trigger must be released and enter a new gate within 10 seconds before flap starts to move.
