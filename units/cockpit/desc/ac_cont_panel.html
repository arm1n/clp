AC Control Pane

The ac control panel gives manual control of the ac variable frequency system. It has two toggle switches for generators on and off control and one toggle switch for ac externalpower.

The toggle switches are used to open and close ac variablefrequency system electrical circuits.The switches are identified as GEN 1, GEN 2, and EXT POWER.

The three switches have an OFF label to show their off position.
