WX Radar Control Pane

WXR system related controls (i.e. WXR display brightness, range selection, arc/full navigation display mode selection) are controlled by the Electronic Flight Instrument System (EFIS) and are accessible to each flight crew member by their related EFIS Control Panel (EFCP) and Weather Radar Control Panel (WXCP). Each pilot has the option of setting the range independent of the other pilot.

The Weather Radar supplies data to the systems that follow:
  • Pilot and copilot Multi Function Displays (MFD1, MFD2)
  • Input/Output Processors (IOP1, IOP2)
