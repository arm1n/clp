DOOR REMOTE CONTROL PANEL

The Internal Doors Control Panel is located on the overhead console and has the label “INTERNAL DOORS” inscribed on the face plate. It allows full control and indication of the flight compartment door during flight operations.

  • BAGG DOOR / CKPT DOOR LIGHT (dual lights)
  • ROTARY KNOB (3 position, momentary action)
  • FAIL / AUTO UNLK LIGHT (dual lights)
  • LOCK ISOLATE GUARDED SWITCH LIGHT (two position)
