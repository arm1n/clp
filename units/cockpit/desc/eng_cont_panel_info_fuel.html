Fuel/Engine/Propeller Control-Panel

FUEL TRANSFER SWITCH (three position, lever latched)

  • TO TANK 1
      • activates transfer system to transfer fuel from tank No. 2 to tank No. 1

  • CENTER
      • transfer system deactivated

  • TO TANK 2
      • activates transfer system to transfer fuel from tank No. 1 to tank No. 2

TANK 1/2 AUX PUMP SWITCHLIGHT

  • PUSH - ON segment (green)
      • activates auxiliary fuel pump in left or right collector bay

  • PUSH - ON segment (out)
      • deactivates auxiliary fuel pump in left or right collector bay

  • ON segment (green) without pushing
      • auxiliary fuel pump has been activated by the fuel transfer system (TANK to TANK)
