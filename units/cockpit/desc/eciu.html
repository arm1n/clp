ENGINE COCKPIT INTERFACE UNIT (ECIU)

The engine cockpit interface unit (ECIU) transfers data between the cockpit and both full authority digital engine controllers (FADECs). It contains a single main circuit card assembly (CCA) that consists of two redundant channels (A and B).

The ECIU receives 24 inputs per channel from the cockpit that are transmitted to the FADEC and 16 inputs per channel from the engines that are transmitted to the cockpit.
