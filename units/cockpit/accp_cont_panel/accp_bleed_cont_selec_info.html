BLEED CONTROL SELECTOR (rotary action)

  • signals ECU to modulate the nacelle shut-off valves
  • provides air flow for 1 of 3 selected settings (for engine bleed operation)

MIN - allows Environmental Control System (ECS) controller to modulate the amount of bleed air from both engines at a minimum level.

NORM - allows ECS controller to modulate the amount of bleed air from both engines at increased levels (for moderate bleed air flow).

MAX - allows ECS controller to modulate the amount of bleed air from both engines at increased levels (for maximum bleed air flow).
