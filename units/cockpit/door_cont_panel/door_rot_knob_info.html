ROTARY KNOB (3 position, momentary action)

  • UNLK (momentary action) - push knob in and rotate left
      • - Solenoid will de-activate and unlatch flight compartment door

  • AUTO - normal position
      • - light illuminates if flight compartment door is unlatched

  • DENY (momentary action) - rotate knob right
      • - Cancels auto-unlock sequence and terminates automatic unlatching of flight compartment door.
