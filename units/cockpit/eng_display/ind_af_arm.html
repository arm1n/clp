A/F ARM MESSAGE

The autofeather function is armed by moving the PLA to above 60 and by having local and remote torques above 50% and pressing the AUTOFEATHER SELECT switch. When both powerplants are armed. an A/F ARM indication is shown on the Engine Display (ED).
