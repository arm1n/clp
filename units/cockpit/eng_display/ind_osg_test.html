OSG TEST

indicates the status of the prop overspeed governor or autofeather test

The single wound solenoid is energized by operating the OSG Test Switch on the pilot's side console. An interlock to prevent operation in flight is provided by the WOW input to a low side switch in the PEC.

As soon as the test is launched the OSG TEST or A/F TEST (white) message appears followed by the following messages:

  • IN PROG in white, or
  • ABORT or FAILED in yellow, or
  • PASSED in green, depending on the status of the test

Nothing is displayed when the test conditions are removed or when no valid data are received
