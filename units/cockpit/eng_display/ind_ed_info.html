Engine Display, ED

The Engine and System Integrated Display (ESID) system shows the additional system pages that follows:
  • Engine
  • Fuel
  • Doors

The Engine Display (ED) is located at the center of the instrument panel. It shows primary engine and aircraft systems data that follows:

  • Torque
  • NH High Pressure Compressor Rotational Speed
  • NP Propeller Rotational Speed
  • ITT Interâˆ’Turbine Temperature
  • Fuel Flow
  • NL Low Pressure Compressor Rotational Speed
  • Engine rating mode
  • Oil Pressure
  • Oil Temperature
  • Tank Fuel Quantity
  • Fuel inlet temperature
  • Advisory messages
  • Static Air Temperature
  • System Page (Mono Mode)

Three different types of advisory messages are shown in white lettersat the bottom part of the Engine Display (ED). An IFC, powerplant, or displayrelated message is shown at a different location while the condition is present.

The messages are shown in order of decreasing importance. The most importantdisplay message is shown at the bottom right part of the ED. The DUs causethe ED to show the advisory messages that follow:

  • DU BAD CONF
  • ED MON FAIL
  • PFD1,PFD2 MON FAIL
  • HOT PFD1, MFD1, ED, MFD2 or PFD2
  • HOT DISPLAYS
  • FD1, MFD1, MFD2 or PFD2 LINK FAIL
