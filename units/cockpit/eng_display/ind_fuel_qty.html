FUEL QUANTITY INDICATION

The Engine Indicating System (EIS) in the flight compartment displays the fuel quantity information to the flight crew. The fuel quantity information is also displayed on the Refuel/Defuel Indicator (RDI) located in the refuel/defuel panel.

(Digital value white, yellow if imbalance, LBS or KG in blue)

  • total fuel quantity given in LBS or KG
  • indicates from 0 to 9990 in 10 LBS or 5 KG increments
  • digits are replaced by white dashes when the data is not valid
