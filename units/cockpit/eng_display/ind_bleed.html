BLEED

  • With the BLEED switches on, and NTOP or MTOP set, indicates BLEED (white) on the Engine Display (ED)
  • With the BLEED switches on, and NTOP set, indicates BLEED (amber) on the ED
  • With the BLEED switches on, and MTOP set, rating display changes to, and indicates MCP. BLEED is not displayed
