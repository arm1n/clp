STAB (Stabilization, momentary action)

PUSH - The STAB button turns the pitch and roll stability ON and OFF. Attitude stabilization can be turned off when the STAB button is pressed, in which case, the yellow STAB OFF annunciator is dis- played on the MFD in the upper-left corner below the mode field and the tilt value annunciation. Stabilization is re-selected by re-pressing the STAB pushbutton.

NOTE: When in FSBY (Forced Standby) mode, pushing the STAB button 4 times within 3 s allows ground operation of the weather radar system.
