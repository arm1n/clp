TILT knob (rotary action)

TURN - controls the tilt angle of antenna beam with relation to the horizon through a selectable range of ± 15°. The range between + 5° and - 5° is expanded for ease of setting. A digital readout of the indi- cated tilt angle is displayed in cyan.

NOTE: To avoid flying under or over storms, frequently adjust the tilt to scan both above and below your flight level.
