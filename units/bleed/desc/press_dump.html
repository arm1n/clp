Pressure Dump

The fast depressurization function may be done in the automatic and the manual modes. The AUTO/MAN/DUMP switch set to DUMP fully opens the aft outflow valve. In the manual mode, the aft outflow valve opens when the toggle switch is moved and held in the DECR position. In DUMP mode the aft outflow valves stay fully open, preventing the aeroplane from pressurizing. DUMP mode may also be used for maximum smoke evacuation.
