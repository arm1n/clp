Aft Fuselage Duct Leak Overtemperature Switches

Two aft fuselage duct leak overtemperature switches are installed on a bracket on the aft fuselage tee duct. They detect a bleed air leak in the engine or APU bleed air ducting near the air conditioning pack inlet. If the temperature in the ducting is greater than 360 °F (182.2 °C), the switch contacts will open. This sends a signal to the ECU to close the nacelle shutoff valve.

The ECU also sends a fault code to the Central Diagnostic System (CDS) identifying a duct leak.
