Egress Emergency Lights

Four egress lights, one located adjacent to each emergency exit, supply external emergency lighting. The forward passenger door egress light is powered by a battery pack which also powers the related EXIT signs.

The front right emergency exit egress light and EXIT signs are powered by a separate battery pack. The egress lights and EXIT signs for the two aft exits are powered by the same battery pack. The battery pack for the aft exits is located in the ceiling between the two exits.
