ELEVATOR LEFT FORWARD QUADRANT

The left forward quadrant is located below the flight compartment floor. The quadrant transmits fore and aft movement of the pilot's control column to the left aft quadrant.

The stick pusher quadrant is directly attached to the left forward quadrant with input from the stick pusher actuator.

A rig pin point is located in the quadrant.

Regulators, Elevator Tension Tension regulator springs installed in each of the forward quadrants give correct tension of the elevator control cables. The elevator tension regulators adjust the cable tension according to ambient temperature.
