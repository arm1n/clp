Functional Display

A rudder position LVDT is located in the vertical stabilizer next to the rudder. It monitors the position of the rudder then transmits the rudder position data to the Flight Data Recorder (FDR). The rudder position LVDT also transmits the rudder position data to the Powered Flight Control Surface Indicator (PFSCI) located on the pilot's Multi Functional Display (MFD) to display the position of the rudder.
