CAUTION LIGHTS

An ELEVATOR ASYMMETRY caution light comes on when there is a difference between the left and right elevator positions The ELEV FEEL caution light, controlled by the FCECU, comes on if:- Left or right Pitch Feel Actuator fails- Airspeed No.1 and Airspeed No.2 disagree- Acceleration No.1 and Acceleration No.2 disagree. An ELEVATOR PRESS caution light comes on if all three systems are supplying power to the elevator actuators. The #3 HYD PUMP caution light comes on if the #3 Hyd system pressure falls to 900 psi (6205 kPa), or the pump has been operating for longer than 60 seconds on the ground.
