Pitch Feel and Trim Units (PFTUs)

Two identical Pitch Feel and Trim Units (PFTUs), left and right, are located in the vertical stabilizer. They supply both pitch feel and pitch trim inputs to the elevator control system. The right PFTU is in parallel with the copilot's elevator controls and the left PFTU is in parallel with the pilot's elevator controls. Centering springs help to return the elevator control surfaces to a neutral position

The elevator feel and trim system has the components that follow:



  • Units, Pitch Feel and Trim
  • Actuators, Pitch Trim
  • Actuators, Pitch Feel
  • Indicator, Elevator Trim
