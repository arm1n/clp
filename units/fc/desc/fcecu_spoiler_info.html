Spoiler Control System

The FCECU does the functions that follow:
- Locks out the outboard spoilers for airspeeds greater than 170 kt (315 km/h).
- Operates both the inboard and outboard spoilers to dump the lift from the wings when the aircraft lands.
- Sends a signal to the spoiler caution and warning lights to indicate system malfunctions
- Supplies electrical excitation of the inboard and outboard spoiler position LVDTs
- Receives position data from the inboard and outboard spoiler LVDTs and transmits the data to the FDR and EFIS by way of the IFC.
