ELEVATOR LEFT AFT QUADRANT

The left aft quadrant is located in the vertical stabilizer. The quadrant is made of aluminum and rotates on a sealed bearing. It transmits the pilot's pitch control commands from the left forward quadrant through a series of pushrods, torque tubes and linkages to the left elevator PCUs.

The elevator autopilot quadrant is directly attached to the left aft quadrant with input from the autopilot servo. A rig pin point is located in the quadrant.
