ELEVATOR PCU INPUT BUNGEE

The elevator control system has eight overload bungees. Two bungees are located in the elevator control circuit; one between the right aft quadrant and its related torque tube and the other between the left aft quadrant and its related torque tube.

The other six overload bungees are installed in series between the two PCU input torque tubes and the six elevator PCUs. The overload bungees contract or expand to compensate for structural deflection or thermal expansion or contraction. This prevents structural damage to the elevator surfaces or the surrounding structure. The overload bungees also prevent any damage to the elevator control circuit in the event of a jam in one or more of the elevator PCUs.
