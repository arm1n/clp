CAUTION LIGHTS

The #1 STALL SYST FAIL and #2 STALL SYST FAIL caution lights come on immediately in flight when malfunctions that prevent stick shaker and stick pusher operation are sensed. Malfunctions of non-critical parameters are not shown in flight.

The #1 STALL SYST FAIL or #2 STALL SYST FAIL caution light will come on 30 seconds after the aircraft has landed. A stick shaker motor malfunction is not shown in flight. The #1 STALL SYST FAIL or #2 STALL SYST FAIL caution light will come on 30 seconds after the aircraft has landed.

Any of the conditions that follow will cause the PUSHER SYST FAIL caution light to come on:
  • Any failure that causes the #1 STALL SYST FAIL or #2 STALL SYST FAIL caution lights to turn on
  • A failure of the stick pusher actuator

The PUSHER SYST FAIL caution light comes on when a stick pusher SHUT OFF switchlight selection is made. The PUSHER SYST FAIL light will go out when the two stick pusher SHUT OFF switchlights are not set and no malfunctions exist.
