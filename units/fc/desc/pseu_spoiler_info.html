Proximity Sensor Electronic-Unit

Ground Mode: With the engine power levers at or below flight idle plus 12 degrees and the two Weight-On-Wheels signals (WOW1 and WOW2) supplied from the Proximity Sensor Electronic Unit (PSEU), the FCECU energizes all the lift dump valves. All four spoiler panels will fully extend and the lift made by the wings will decrease. The FLIGHT/TAXI switch must be in the FLIGHT position.
