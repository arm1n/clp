ELEVATOR RIGHT FORWARD QUADRANT

The right forward quadrant is located below the flight compartment floor. The quadrant transmits fore and aft movement of the copilot's control column to the right aft quadrant.

A rig pin point is located in the quadrant.

Regulators, Elevator Tension Tension regulator springs installed in each of the forward quadrants give correct tension of the elevator control cables. The elevator tension regulators adjust the cable tension according to ambient temperature.
