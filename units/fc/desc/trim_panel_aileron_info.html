Aileron Trim Panel

The pilot controls the amount of aileron trim by operating the aileron trim switch, which supplies the electrical signal to the aileron trim actuator. Selection of the LWD position moves the left aileron up and the right aileron down. Selection of the RWD position moves the left aileron down and the right aileron up.

The AILERON trim switch has three positions:
- LWD (left wing down)
- RWD (right wing down)
- Center-off

The switch is spring-loaded to return to the center-off position.
