Linear Variable Differential Transformer (LVDT)

Linear Variable Differential Transformer (LVDT), Spoiler Position Refer to Figure 17. and Figure 18.The LVDT gives position indication through the FCECU to the Powered Flight Controls Surface Indicator (PFCSI), in the flight compartment and to the Flight Data Recorder (FDR). The LVDT is attached to a bracket on the aft face of the wing rear spar and a bracket on the spoiler panel.
