Rudder Aft Quadrant

The rudder aft quadrant is located halfway up the vertical stabilizer next to the Power Control Unit. It is the last quadrant in the rudder control system. The rudder aft quadrant transmits rudder control inputs from the mixer quadrant to the Power Control Units. The rudder aft quadrant has the primary travel stops for the rudder cable system. Rotation of the aft quadrant is opposed by two feel springs, which supply an artificial feel to the rudder pedals.
