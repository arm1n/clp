Rudder shut-off valves

The Rudder shut-off valve are normaly energised, porting 3000 psi supply pressure to the upper and lower PCUÂ´s.

If a PCU pressure regulation failure is detected, the FCECU will command zero pressure to the PCU pressure control valve and command the associated rudder sov to close.
