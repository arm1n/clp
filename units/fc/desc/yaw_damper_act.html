Yaw damper actuator

A rudder yaw damper actuator, supplies automatic compensation for minor rudder deflections to improve directional stability and turn coordination.The yaw damper actuator works independently or in conjunction with the Automatic Flight Control System (AFCS). Yaw damper authority is 4.5 degrees of rudder movement either side of centre. The yaw damper gets its inputs from Flight Guidance Modules N0.1 and No.2 and needs both inputs for operation
