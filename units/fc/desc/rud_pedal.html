RUDDER PEDALS

Movement of the rudder pedals supplies yaw commands to the rudder control system. The yaw commands are mechanically transmitted from the pilot's and copilot's rudder pedals to the rudder surfaces. These commands are transmitted through the Rudder Feel and Trim Unit and the rudder Power Control Units (PCUs). The PCUs move the rudder surfaces using hydraulic power.


Rudder Adjustment Pedals

A rudder pedal adjustment handle allows fore and aft adjustment of either pair of rudder pedals to accommodate individual pilot preference for pedal difference. Each adjustment handle is located on the pedestal between the pilot's feet.
