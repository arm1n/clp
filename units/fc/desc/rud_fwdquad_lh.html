Rudder Forward L/H Quadrant

There are two rudder forward quadrants, one located below the pilot's rudder pedals and the other located below the copilot's rudder pedals.

Each rudder forward quadrant is connected to its applicable pair of rudder pedals through a series of tubes and levers. The rudder forward quadrant transmits rudder cable control movement from the rudder pedals aft to the mixer quadrant.
