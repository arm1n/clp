ELEVATOR ANNUNCIATION AREA

The Linear Variable Differential Transformer (LVDT) is a single channel, single sensor LVDT. It senses the elevator surface position and transmits the position data to the Flight Control Electronic Control Unit (FCECU) and to the Powered Flight Control Surface Indicator (PFCSI) on the pilots MFD. There are two LVDTs located in the horizontal stabilizer; one attached to each elevator surface.
