Low Oil Pressure Switch:

The switch is located on the forward face of the gearbox assembly and senses oil pressure, downstream of the oil filter.

The normally closed switch opens when oil pressure exceeds 35 psig (241 kPag), the switch will close when oil pressure decreases below this value during APU operation.

An open switch prior to start (failed switch), or a closed switch during operation (failed switch or low pressure) will result in the FADEC automatically shutting down the APU.
