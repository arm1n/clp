Magnetic Drain Plug:

A magnetic drain plug is located on the lower, front side of the gearbox housing.

When the plug is removed, oil is drained from the oil sump. The drain plug has a magnetic chip detector that attracts ferrous metal particles in the oil.

The detector may be removed, inspected, and installed without draining the oil sump. A check valve located inside the drain plug prevents oil from draining when the chip detector is removed.
