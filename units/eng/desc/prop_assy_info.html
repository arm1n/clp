PROPELLER BLADE / PROP HARNESS

Electrical Continuity Check of the Propeller Deicing Heater Element
AMM TASK 30-61-00-760-801 (TR-AMM)

STEP 1
  • Disconnect the connector from the Brush Block Unit

STEP 2
  • The inner (PH A) ring to middle (PH B) ring. (BLADE 1 and 4)
    (The resistance must be between 10 and 12 ohms)

  • The inner (PH A) ring to outer (PH C) ring. (BLADE 3 and 6)
    (The resistance must be between 10 and 12 ohms)

  • The middle (PH B) ring to outer (PH C) ring. (BLADE 2 and 5)
    (The resistance must be between 10 and 12 ohms)

STEP 3
  • Resistance check at the blade assembly.
    (The resistance must be between 30.1 and 33.2 ohms)
