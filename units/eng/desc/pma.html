Permanent Magnet Alternator (PMA)

The permanent magnet alternator supplies electrical power to the FADEC and Propeller Electronic Control (PEC).

The PMA supplies sufficient electrical power for FADEC at 20% Nh and sufficient power for PEC at 40% Nh.

The FADEC selects the higher voltage from these alternative supplies:
  • PMA
  • Essential bus

In the event of PMA supply failure, the FADEC automatically reverts to essential bus power.
