Ceiling Panels And Covering, Passenger Compartment

On pre NextGen aircraft, the ceiling panels are the same shape as the top of the aircraft structure. They are made of two layers of glass reinforced plastic with a decorative cover. The panels are installed with spring clips on the top left edge and with screws on the right edge.

On NextGen aircraft, the ceiling is covered with the ceiling panels and transition panels. The emergency flood lights are installed on the ceiling panels. The ceiling panels and the transition panels are of the same shape as the top of the aircraft structure.
