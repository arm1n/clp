The Stall Protection Modules (SPM1, SPM2)

The Stall Protection Module (SPM1, SPM2) has the two electronic circuit boards that follow:
  • Extension board
  • Central Processing Unit (CPU) board

One part of the extension board is for the Stall Protection System (SPS) and the other part is used by Ground Proximity Warning System (GPWS) for input/output processing.The SPM uses discrete signals and ARINC 429 interfaces between the Central Processing Unit (CPU) and external boards. The SPM make a reference voltage for AOA transducer resolvers that is 7 Vac, 2500 Hz.

The Stall Protection Modules (SPM1, SPM2) transmit data to the components that follow:
  • Flight Guidance Module (FGM1,FGM2)
  • Stick shakers
  • Stick pusher
  • Caution and Warning Annunicator Lights Panel
  • Flight Data Concentrator (FDC)
  • Ground Proximity Warning System (GPWS)
  • Central Diagnostics System (CDS)

The SPMs are Line Replaceable Modules (LRM) that are located in their related IFC.
