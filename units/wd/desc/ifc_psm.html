Prime Power Supply Module (PPSM 1, PPSM 2)

The PPSM receives 28 Vdc from the main and essential busses. The PPSM has the functions that follow:
  • Filtering
  • Pre-regulation
  • Energy reserve
  • Power limitaion
The modules that normally receive power are as follows:
  • SPM 1/SPM 2
  • IOM 1/IOM 2
  • IOP 1/IOP 2
  • FGM 1/FGM 2
The modules that receive emergency power are as follows
  • IOM 1
  • IOP 1
  • FGM 1
