Remote Control Audio Unit (RCAU)

The RCAU supplies pilot, copilot, observer, and passenger address headphone and microphone audio data to the SSCVR. The microphone audio does not have to be transmitted for it to be recorded. The audio that is sent to the SSCVR represents the audio and volume selections of the ARCDUs and ACP.

The RCAU is used to control the AIS from ARCDU or observerâ€™s ACP selections.

NOTE: A red FAIL message is shown in the interphone display area to show that the RCAU is failed.
