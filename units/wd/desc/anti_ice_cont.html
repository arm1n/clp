Controllers, Anti−icing

Two Anti−Ice Controllers (AICs) control heater operation to prevent ice accumulating on the windshields and the pilot’s side window. The AICs activate the heaters using relays. The relays are used to isolate the AICs from the high current flow required for heating.

When the windshield control is in the OFF position, the deactivation relay is controlled using the overheat temperature sensor. The activation relay stays open regardless of the normal temperature sensorinformation. In the WARM−UP or NORM modes, the Anti−Ice Controller (AIC) uses the normal temperature sensor information to control the activation relay and the overheat temperature sensor to control the deactivation relay.
