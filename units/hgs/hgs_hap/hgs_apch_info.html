Indicates that an approach warning has occurred. The APCH WARN annunciation flashes for 5 seconds then remains steady on. NO AIII and APCH WARN show concurrently on the Combiner.

An approach warning occurs below 500 feet AGL if an AIII capability fault occurs or if the approach monitor detects an out of tolerance approach. Approach warnings are recorded in fault history, which can be viewed using the HGS MAINTENANCE MENU.

The APCH WARN light comes on under one of these conditions:

  • The approach monitor determines there is an out-oftolerance condition
  • The HGS has lost AIII capability (see AIII Status)
  • The Autopilot is engaged below 500 feet
