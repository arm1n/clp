THE MULTI−FUNCTION DISPLAY ELECTRICAL PAGE

The multi−function displays located on the pilot and co−pilot instrument panels will display the electrical page when selected on the ESID control panel MFD reversion switches.

The electrical page shows the AC and DC generation system indications that follow:

  • Digital indication of the ac generator voltage of the A, B, and C phases
  • Digital indication of ac generator load of the A, B, and C phases
  • AC EXT Power

  • Digital indication of the dc essential, main, and secondary bus voltages
  • Digital indication of the dc essential, main, and standby voltages
  • Digital indication of dc starter/generator loads − Digital indication of TRU loads
  • Digital indication of APU loads
  • DC EXT Power
