LEFT GENERATOR BUS CONTACTOR K1

A two-position center-off bus contactor with a 150 A three-pole double throw (3PDT) contact arrangement and associated auxiliary contacts.

Two separate coil windings are used to move the contact arms to connect the ac busses to their related or the opposite generator (cross-tie position).

The contactor arms also move to the cross-tie position when the external ac power is selected.
