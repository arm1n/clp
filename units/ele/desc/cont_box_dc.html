DC Contactor Box (DCCB)

The components in the DCCB give control and distribution of the DC power source from the two DC starter/generators transformer rectifier units, main and auxiliary batteries, or DC external power source.

The DCCB contains the components that follow:

  • DC starter/generator bus tie contactors, K1 and K2
  • Main feeder bus tie contactor, K21
  • Secondary feeder bus tie contactor, K22
  • Secondary/main feeder bus tie contactors, K5 and K6
  • DC external power contactor, K9
  • Battery bus tie contactor, K7 and K8
  • Diodes
  • Current transformers
  • DC circuit breakers
  • Protective fuses
