DC CONTACTOR K9

External power is connected to the aircraft busses through contactor K9 if:
  • Battery master toggle switch set to the on position,and
  • External pwr toggle switch set to the on position,and
  • DC and APU GCU not sense an overcurrent condition
The EPCU checks the external power source for the parameters thatfollow:
