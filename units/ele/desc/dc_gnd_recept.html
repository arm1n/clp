EXTERNAL DC POWER

When the DC external power toggle switch located on the DC control panel is set to EXT PWR, the DC external power source is connected to the main, essential, and secondary DC busses.


The DC external power condition is sensed by the EPCU and it operates as follows:
  • The external power contactor K9 connects the dc external power source to the left main bus
  • The main bus tie contactor K21 connects the left main feeder bus to the right main feeder bus
  • The secondary/main feeder bus contactors, K5 and K6 connect the secondary feeder busses to the main feeder busses.
  • An inhibit signal is sent from the EPCU to the DC GCUs to open the bus contactors K1, K2 and to de-energize the DC starter/generator while external power is selected

The EPCU checks the external power source for the parameters that follow:
Parameter Trip Point Over Voltage 31 Vdc Under Voltage 22 Vdc Polarity Reversed
