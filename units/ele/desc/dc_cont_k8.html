DC CONTACTOR K8

The K8 contactor connects the main battery to the right main feeder bus. The contactor is closed by switching 28 Vdc through the battery master and main battery toggle switches.

The contactor is prevented from closing if the EPCU senses an over current condition sent by the opposite DC GCU for 1 second or from the related DC GCU for 5 seconds or an emergency condition is sensed.

NOTE: The EPCU has an electromechanical relay with normally closed contacts to bypass the EPCU and let the pilots operate K8 when the EPCU is de-energized.
