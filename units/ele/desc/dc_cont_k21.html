DC CONTACTOR K21

K21 is activated closed when the DC bus tie toggle switch input to the EPCU is energized. This function is locked out by the EPCU when a bus fault is sensed. A reset is done by supplying a reset signal to the SPCU.

K21 is also controlled automatically by the EPCU to tie the main feeder buses if:
  • No overcurrent is sensed by the DC GCUs, and
  • Bus tie toggle switch is set, or
  • One DC starter/generator is defective, or
  • DC external power is supplied, or
  • APU power is supplied
