DC CONTACTOR K5

Contactors K5 and K6 connect the main feeder busses to the secondary feeder busses.

The contactors close if:
  • Two DC starter/gen. malfunction, two TRU's available
      • - OR -
  • Two TRUs malfunction, two DC starter/gen. available
      • - OR -
  • DC Ext. Pwr. is available and both TRUs are defective
      • - OR -
  • APU starter/generator power is available
      • - OR -
  • No over current condition is sensed and a start selection is not made
