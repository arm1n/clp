Electro pneumatic shut−off valve

The solenoid operated valve is installed on the relief port line of the forward passenger door seal pressurization valve.

The primary function of this electro pneumatic shut−off valve is to prevent depressurization of the door seal system if airspeed is more than 100 knots (airspeed > 100 knots). This feature prevents inadvertent door seal depressurization for the forward passenger door.
