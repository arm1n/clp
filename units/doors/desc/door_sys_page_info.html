Doors Warning System Indication

When any door is open and unlocked the indications that follow will be shown:

  • The Doors Systems page shows the open door as a red filled rectangle
  • The FUSELAGE DOORS warning light flashes
  • The MASTER WARNING light flashes

A closed and locked door is shown as green empty rectangle on the Doors System page. An open door is shown as a red filled rectangle with a legend identifying the door in red T1 font.

If data global from both IOPs is invalid, the Doors System page will show a white “INVALID DATA” message.
