Valve, Charging

The manually operated charging valve allows the system to be pressurized from an external source. This allows opening and closing of the two doors without operating an engine.

After maintenance the reservoir can be pressurized on the ground, through the charging valve located in the Forward Lower Wardrobe Forward Access panel on aircraft with PRE MODSUM 4−153583. On aircraft with POST MODSUM 4−153583 through the charging valve located below the floor at the forward passenger door entrance.
