Reser Electrical Schematic

The No.1 and No.2 hydraulic systems each contain a 225 F (107 C) and a 275 F (135 C) temperature switch, installed on the hydraulic system reservoir.

The switches monitor the temperature of the hydraulic fluid inside the reservoir. If fluid temperature reaches 225 F (107 C) the switch sends a signal to the No.1 or No.2 HYD FLUID HOT caution light.

If fluid temperature reaches 275 F (135 C), the temperature switch sends a signal which closes the firewall shut-off valve.
