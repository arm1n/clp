Hydraulic System Ecology Bottles

The No.1 hydraulic system ecology bottle and the SPU ecology bottle are installed in the No.1/left side engine nacelle.

The No.1 ecology bottle collects leakage from the No.1 reservoir bleed/relief valve and the balanced relief valve.


The SPU ecology bottle collects leakage from the SPU shaft seal.

The bottle has a fluid capacity of 0.793 US qt (750 millileters).
