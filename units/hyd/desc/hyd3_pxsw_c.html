System PX Switches

Operates the ELEV PRESS caution light on the Caution and Warning panel if all three systems are supplying power to the elevator actuators.

NOTE:
The CAWP ELEVATOR PRESS caution light comes on when all three hydraulic systems have high pressure.

The ELEVATOR PRESS caution light can come on for conditions that are not malfunctions as follows:

  • No. 1 and No. 2 engines are operating, and
  • The HYD #3 ISOL VALVE annunciator switch is set to OPEN
