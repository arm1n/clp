Firewall Shut-off Valves

The hydraulic firewall Shut-Off Valve (SOV) are installed in the No.1 engine nacelles, in the EDP suction line. This SOV has an electrically operated ball-type shut-off valve and an open/closed position indicator.

The SOVs stop the flow of hydraulic fluid to the EDP if the following conditions exist:
  • Fire
  • Empty hydraulic reservoir fluid quantity
  • Hydraulic fluid over-temperature condition
System 1 (2) FWSOV Control Logic
The System 1 (2) FWSOV (left nacelle) will be commanded closed when:
  • LH (RH) engine PULL FUEL OFF handle is pulled or
  • LH (RH) Reservoir 225°F temperature switch indicates high temperature and
    LH (RH) Reservoir 275°F temperature switch indicates high temperature or
  • LH Reservoir quantity switch indicates empty

If none of the above conditions are satisfied, the System 1 (2) FWSOV will becommanded open.
