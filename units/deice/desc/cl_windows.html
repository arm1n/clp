CAUTION LIGHTS

If either AIC fails, the WSHLD CTRL caution light will come on. Failure messages are recorded in the Timer and Monitor Unit (TMU) for maintenance purposes. Two maintenance messages related to the failures are RIGHT AIC FAILRIGHT AIC FAIL and LEFT AIC FAIL.

An overheat condition of the pilot’s Side Window is shown by the SIDE WDO HOT caution light coming on.

An overheat condition of the pilot’s or copilot’s windshield is shown by the WSHLD HOT caution light coming on.
