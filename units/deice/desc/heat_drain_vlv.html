Automatic Drain Valves

The Automatic Drain Valves (ADV) collect water that has condensed in the system.The valve drains the water when the pressure in the system is below 3 psig (20.7 kPag) at engine shutdown.

When pressure in the system is above 3 psig (20.7 kPag), the valve closes to avoid leakage.The ADV is heated to prevent trapped water from freezing and restricting flow.Two are located in the tail and one in each wing upstream of the two DDV.
