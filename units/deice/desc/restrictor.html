Restrictor

The restrictor lets air flow from one side of the aft system to the other side.

It provides the necessary communication between the two parts of the system to ensure that the tail is correctly de−iced in the event of a failure upstream of one of the Heated Check Valves (HCVs).

It also reduces the amount of air loss in case of a failure downstream of one of the HCVs, which allows partial de−icing of the tail.
