Overheat Relay (K4)



In the event the normal heating control fails, the AIC uses the overheat temperature sensor to control the heater.

In an overheat (temperature above 122 ±6 °F (50 ±3 °C) for windshield or above 126 ±6 °F (52 ±3 °C) for the pilot’s side window), the AIC deactivates both relays and shuts off the heating.
