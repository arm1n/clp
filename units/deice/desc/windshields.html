Windshields




Electrical Resistance Check of the Windshields:

This check only applies when the outside air temperature is between +18.3°C (+65°F) and +23.9°C (+75°F). The aircraft is not in the sunlight.

In an overheat (temperature above 122 ±6 °F (50 ±3 °C) for windshield or above 126 ±6 °F (52 ±3 °C) for the pilot’s side window), the AIC deactivates both relays and shuts off the heating.

NOTE:
To get accurate measurements it is maybe necessary to simulate ambient flight conditions by attaching cooling packs on sensor area.
