Alternate Landing Gear Selector Valve

The LANDING GEAR ALTERNATE EXTENSION door, on the flight compartment floor, must then be fully opened giving access to the alternate extension handpump and the NOSE L/G RELEASE handle. The door operates the alternate landing gear selector valve and reveals the handpump socket.

When the NOSE L/G RELEASE handle is pulled, the nose gear uplock and doors are released and the nose gear free falls to a down and locked position, assisted by the airflow.
