Alternate Indication System

An alternative indication of landing gear extension is given by the visual indicators. The visual indicators are installed in the flight compartment behind the LANDING GEAR ALTERNATE EXTENSION door.

Verification of the alternate indication of the landing gear down and locked condition is as follows:

  • The LANDING GEAR ALTERNATE EXTENSION door in the flight compartment floor is opened
  • The landing gear downlock verification switch is set
  • The three green landing gear downlock verification lights are monitored. If the landing gear is down and locked, all three lights will be on
