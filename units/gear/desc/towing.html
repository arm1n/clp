Towing of the Aircraft

Before you connect the tow bar to the aircraft make sure that the nosewheel steering switch is set to the off position and stays off during towing.

Engage the ground lock of the nose landing gear

Install the lock pins in the main landing gear

After towing, make sure the nosewheel is centered before you disconnect the towbar. If you do not do this, you can cause injuries to persons and/or damage to the equipment.
