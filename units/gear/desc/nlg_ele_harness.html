Nose landing gear electrical harness

Four electrical harnesses are attached at different places on the shock strut. Two additional electrical harnesses are attached to the drag strut.

The four harnesses on the shock strut give power/signal input for the components that follow:

  • The taxi light
  • The WOW proximity sensors
  • The steering proximity sensor
  • The steering manifold pressure switches
  • The Rotary Variable Differential Transformers (RVDTs)

The two harnesses on the drag strut supply electrical power/signal input for the primary and alternate lock proximity sensors.
