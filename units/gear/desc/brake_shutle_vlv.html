Fuse/Shuttle Valve

There are four fuse/shuttle valves installed on the left wall of the left and the right nacelles.

Hydraulic pressure is supplied from the No.1 hydraulic system to the brake units through the skid control valves and the fuse/shuttle valves.

The fuse component of the fuse/shuttle valve closes the hydraulic line to the brake unit, if the fluid flow is more than a specified quantity. This will limit the leakage of hydraulic fluid if a brake line on the main landing gear is cut or if a brake unit develops a leak.

The shuttle component of the fuse/shuttle valve allows either normal brake pressure or parking/emergency brake pressure to be used to actuate the brake units.
