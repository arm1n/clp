Extension bypass valve

The bypass valve is installed in the forward right wing root area, adjacent to the landing gear selector valve.

The bypass valve isolates the No. 2 hydraulic supply lines from the main and nose gear retraction actuators, and connects the lines to the return lines.

The bypass valve is actuated when the LANDING GEAR ALTERNATE RELEASE door is opened. Cables extend from the LANDING GEAR ALTERNATE RELEASE door and attach to a lever at the bypass valve. The lever operates the bypass valve.
