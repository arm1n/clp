Normal Braking System

Each main wheel is equipped with a multiple disc brake unit powered by the No. 1 hydraulic system . An Anti Skid Control Unit (ASCU) modulates the application of brake pressure to each brake unit. Brake pressure is applied by pushing the pilot’s or copilot’s brake pedals.

The anti skid control unit has the functions that follow:

  • Monitors wheel speed
  • Modulates the brake pressure applied to each brake unit to prevent wheel lock-up
  • Gives maximum braking at all levels of runway friction
