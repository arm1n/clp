Nose landing gear centering mechanism and Proximity Sensors

The nosewheel centering mechanism is installed on the lower end of the NLG inner cylinder. The spring loaded centering actuator has a roller bearing that rolls on a machined race on the NLG outer cylinder. The race has a detent at the centered position to return the nosewheels to the straight ahead position.

The centering mechanism centers the nosewheels when the landing gear is retracted.
