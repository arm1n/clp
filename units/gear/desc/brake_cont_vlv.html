Brake control valve

The brake control valve is installed in the left wing root on the front spar.

The pilot’s and copilot’s left brake pedals are connected to the left lever of the brake control valve. The pilot’s and copilot’s right brake pedals are connected to the right lever of the brake control valve.

The brake control valve has two internal metering sections in a common housing. Each metering section is actuated progressively by an integral lever mechanism assembly.

Hydraulic pressure and return lines connect the brake control valve to the No.1 hydraulic system.

Hydraulic lines connect the brake control valve to the anti−skid valves.
