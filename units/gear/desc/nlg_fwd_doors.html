Nose landing gear forward doors

The two NLG forward doors are hinged to the fuselage at two places. The forward hinges form the attachment brackets for the operating mechanism.

The operating mechanism has two adjustable connecting rods, an extension spring, a bellcrank, a toggle link assembly, a link assembly, a mechanical sequence valve, and a hydraulic actuator. This operating mechanism is attached to the forward wall of the nosewheel bay and is protected by a removable debris guard.

Each door is made up of an inner skin and an outer skin. The inner skin is contoured to give clearance for the nosewheels in the retracted position.
