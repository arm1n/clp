Main landing gear drag strut

The drag strut is a fixed length tubular component and attached at the lower end to the shock strut outer cylinder and at the upper end to the airframe structure of the forward section of the wheelwell.

A bracket assembly near the top end of the drag strut is to attach to the linkages of the forward doors of the wheelwell. Brackets attach the electrical harnesses for the proximity sensors and wheel speed transducers, and hydraulic pipes for the brake units along the rear of the drag strut.

A grounding point to ground the aircraft during maintenance procedures is fitted to the top of the drag strut.
