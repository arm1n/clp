NOSE GEAR Proximity Sensors

There are two NLG Down proximity sensors installed in the NLG wheel whell. A target is installed on the right and left hand side of the NLG upper drag strut.


NOSE GEAR LOCK Proximity Sensors

There are 2 NLG Lock Proximity Sensors installed at the backside of the NLG drag strut.

Together with 2 NLG Down proximity sensors the NLG Down and Locked condition can be verified.
