Gear System - Caution Lights

If a landing gear hydraulic sequencing valve fails, or the PSEU is unable to control it, the LDG GEAR INOP caution light comes on. The landing gear selector must not be used to extend the landing gear when the LDG GEAR INOP caution light is on.

The CAWP shows the OUTBD/INBD ANTISKID light on when there is a loss of ability to command or control the OUTBD/INBD antiskid system. OUTBD/INBD antiskid control unit malfunction or ANTISKID toggle switch is OFF.

When a component in the steering system fails, the SCU sends a signal to the Caution and Warning panel. When the signal is received, the NOSE STEERING caution light comes on.

When the parking brake lever is in the PARK position, the PARKING BRAKE caution light on the Caution and Warning panel comes on.
