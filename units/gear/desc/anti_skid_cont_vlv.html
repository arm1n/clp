Anti-Skid control valve

The skid control valves are two separate valves in one common manifold and receive metered pressure from the brake control valve.

The right control valve gives skid control for the inboard and outboard brake units on the right main landing gear.

The left control valve gives skid control for the inboard and outboard brake units on the left main landing gear.

The skid control valves are installed on the left wall of the left and right nacelle.
