Power steering unit

The power steering unit has a steering manifold, a hydraulic motor, and a reduction gearbox with Rotary Variable Differential Transformers (RVDT).

The steering manifold controls the hydraulic pressure to the hydraulic motor. The hydraulic motor operates a reduction gearbox which steers the nosewheels.

Hydraulic pressure to operate the steering system for the nosewheels is supplied by the No.2 hydraulic system.
