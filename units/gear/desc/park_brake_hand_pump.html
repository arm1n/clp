Parking Brake Handpump

When the aircraft is on the ground the parking/emergency brake system accumulator can be pressurized with a handpump installed in the right nacelle.

The accumulator has a nitrogen pre-charge pressure. A lever operated handpump is installed in the right engine nacelle.

It supplies an alternate method to pressurize the accumulator to 3000 psi (20684 kPa) when the aircraft is on the ground and the engines are not operating.
