Alternate LDG Hydraulic Hand Pump

The hand pump is located below the flight compartment floor, adjacent to the copilot seat. The handpump is used to lower the landing gear following System 2 isolation.

The handpump produces 0.75 in³ (12.29 cm³) per stroke.

A hand pump handle is installed in the flight compartment on the bulkhead behind the copilot's seat. The handle must be installed into the pump to operate it.
