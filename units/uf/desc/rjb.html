RELAY JUNCTION BOX

Each RJB contains relays that are used in the switching functions of the various aircraft systems.The junction boxes are installed in the center of the fuselage below the cabin floor, aft of the flight compartment.

Each RJB contains three daughter boards and each daughter board can hold up to four relays. The RJBs interface with other systems through a 100-pin ARINC 600 connector.
