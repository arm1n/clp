Flight Control Electronic Control Unit (FCECU)

The Flight Control Electronic Control Unit (FCECU) receiveselectrical/electronic inputs and supplies electrical/electronic outputs,for all of the primary and secondary flight control systems.The FCECU is a single unit divided into two channels, 1 and 2, (alsoreferred to as the left and right channels). The left and right channelsreceive inputs from the pilot and/or copilot flight control commands. The two channels function separately from each other throughout theentire system, from the FCECU to the individual Line ReplaceableUnits (LRUs) and electrical busses.

The FCECU is part of these Flight Control System (FCS) functions:
  • Rudder Hinge Moment Limiting (HML)
  • Pitch Trim
  • Elevator Feel
  • Spoiler Lift-Dump
  • Outboard Spoiler Lockout
  • Aural Take-Off Warning
  • Flight Control Surface position reporting to the Flight Data Recorder (FDR) and the Powered Flight Control Surface Indicator (PFCSI)
  • Flight Control System Caution and Warning Signals
  • Built-In-Test
  • Maintenance Interface to Central Diagnostic System (CDS)
