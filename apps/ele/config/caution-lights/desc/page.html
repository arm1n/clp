ELECTRICAL POWER - Cautions and Warnings

AC Caution Lights
caution light comes on to show that the generator is not connected to its bus because of a source fault condition, the AC generator toggle switches are set to the OFF position, or external AC power is energizing the left and right busses. caution light comes on to show that the generator temperature is more than 330 °F (166 °C) and goes out when the temperature is less than 305 °F (152 °c). caution light comes on to show that the generator is not connected to its bus because of a bus fault condition. If the bus fault is reset, the indication will go out "OR" caution light also comes on to show that the ac generator contactor is in the center position while the generator is operating and the ac generator toggle switch is set to the ON position.


DC Caution Lights
generator is not connected to its bus because of a source fault condition or toggle switches are set to the OFF position. generator temperature is more than 330° F. show that the a secondary feeder bus is not energized by its related TRU (source fault) or the AC switches are set to the OFF position. TRU temperature is more than 160 °F (71.1 °C) comes on to show that the EPCU has reconfigured the main 28 Vdc generation system because of a source or bus fault condition.. The STBY BATT, AUX BATT or MAIN BATT caution light comes on to show that the battery is not connected to its main feeder bus for charging. The STBY BATT HOT, AUX BATT HOT or MAIN BATT HOT warning light comes on to show that the battery temperature is more than 71 °C. It goes out when the temperature is less than 63 °C.
