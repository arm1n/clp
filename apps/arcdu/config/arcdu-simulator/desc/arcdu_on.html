ARCU MAINTENANCE MODE

The CDS AVIONICS menu shows the MAINTENANCE page on the ARCDU as follows:

  • AVIONICS (L1 Key) Not active in this version

  • OTHER SYSTEMS (L2 Key) All non-avionics systems are accessed through the OTHER SYSTEMS menu.

  • AVIONIC DATA (L3 Key) Not active in this version

Use the NEXT Button to open "OTHER SYSTEM" 2 Page
