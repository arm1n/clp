AILURE IDENTIFICATION:

The door is OPEN and the external door handle is in the CLOSED horizontal position.

NOTE: In this position it not possible to close door

General:

Performing resetting procedure a standard “stand” or “ladder” or "luggage conveyor band" is needed to get access to the external door handle when the door is in the horizontalup (FULL OPEN) position!

At least two persons are required toperform reset procedure.

- STEP 1 -

“Push and hold” lock handle lever assembly to door structure.

HOLD LEVER IN THIS POSITION DURING RESET.


- STEP 2 -

“Lift” the door from the upper stowed position slightly approximately
6-8“ (15-20cm) “up”

HOLD LEVER IN THIS POSITION DURING RESET.

- STEP 3 -

Turn external handle to the
“OPEN POSITION”
(90° in direction indicated)

HOLD LEVER IN THIS POSITION DURING RESET.

- STEP 4 -

Lower the door slowly
Perform door function check to verify reengagement was successful.
