General:

Performing resetting procedure a standard “stand” or “leader” or "luggage conveyor band" is needed to get access to the external door handle when the door is in the horizontalup (FULL OPEN) position!

At least two persons are required toperform reset procedure.

- STEP 1 -

Failure Identification

The door is OPEN and the handle is in the CLOSED position.

Latch arm assembly position is 90° up and the door has
“dropped down”
approximately 1”(2-3 cm).

NOTE:
In this position it not possible to close door.



- STEP 2 -

Warning: Do not touch interlock cam and latch arm assembly during reset.

“Lift” the door slightly approximately 1“ (2-3cm) “up”
HOLD IN THIS POSITION

- STEP 3 -

Turn internal or external handle to the
“open position”
(90° in direction indicated)
AND HOLD IN THIS POSITION

- STEP 4 -

Lower the door slowly

CHECK FOLLOWING

1.

handle is in the “OPEN” positionand

2.

latch arm assembly is in the “horizontal” engaged position
Perform door function check to verify reengagement was successful.
