GS MISMATCH ON PDF

text FIM TASK 34-50-00-810-805 (TR-FIM)

The two FMAs show this message when the flight data processing system (FDPS) senses a very high frequency navigation receiver 1 (VHF NAV 1) and VHF NAV 2 glideslope deviation difference that is more than 25 microamperes.

The PFD electronic horizontal situation indicator (EHSI) and electronic attitude and director indicator (EADI) will not show a related mismatch flag.
