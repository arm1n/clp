ARCDU FAILED ON ARCDU1/2

This fault isolation procedure is for when audio and radio control display unit 1/2 (ARCDU1/2) shows an ARCDU FAILED message.

ARCDU1/2 shows a ARCDU FAILED message when the power-on self-test (POST) senses a discrepancy and will not operate.

NOTE:
The ARCDU can operate in the operational mode if the POST is not started by a short power interruption while the aircraft is on the ground.




NOTE:
  • Switch ARCDU OFF and ON again.

    • Reset all following circuit breakers

  • AVIONICS CB PANEL:
      • C11 COPLT SPKR
      C10 COPLT AUDIO
      C3 COPLT AUDIO
      C9 PLT SPKR
      D4 PLT AUDIO
      C4 OBS AUDIO

  • on PILOT´s CB PANEL
      • H2 PLT AUDIO

  • and on COPILOT´s CB panel.
      • M1 PA EMER PWR
