HEADING MISMATCH message on PFD during flight

FIM TASK 34-20-00-810-802 (TR-FIM)

The FMA shows a HDG MISMATCH message when the flight data processing system (FDPS) senses an attitude and heading reference unit 1 (AHRU1) and AHRU2 heading difference that is more than eight degrees.

NOTE: The FMAs will show only the most important mismatch message and this is the primary mismatch indication.

NOTE: The attitude and heading reference system (AHRS) data must be valid and the EFIS ATT/HDG source reversion selector must be set to the NORM position.








NOTE:

1. Reset AHRU 1 and AHRU 2

  • AHRU 1 (A4) & (A9) Avionics CB
  • AHRU 2 (A3) & (A10) Avionics CB


2. HEADING MISMATCH message ON GROUND

  • Reset AHRU 1 (A4) & (A9) and Avionics CB
      • Flux valve may be distorted by environmental effects (metal objects, dedicated park positions).Move aircraft to other parking position or to taxiway -> Heading mismatch shall be gone.
