LOC MISMATCH

FIM TASK 34-50-00-810-804 (TR-FIM)

The two FMAs show a LOC MISMATCH message when the flight data processing system (FDPS) input/output processor 1 (IOP1) or IOP2 senses a localizer deviation difference.

The PFD electronic horizontal situation indicator (EHSI) and electronic attitude and director indicator (EADI) can also show a related mismatch flag when a very high frequency navigation receiver 1 (VHF NAV 1) and VHF NAV 2 localizer deviation difference is more than 15 microamperes.
