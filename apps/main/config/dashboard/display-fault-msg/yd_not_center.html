YD NOT CENTER


FIM TASK 22-11-00-810-844 (TR-FIM)

This fault isolation procedure is for when the primary flight display (PFD) shows the YD NOT CENTERED advisory message.

The PFD shows the YD NOT CENTERED message when the yaw damper actuator unit (YDAU) is not engaged and not in the centre position.

NOTE: The YD will not move to the center position if there is a malfunction of the :

  • FGM
  • YDAU
  • APAU-R

NOTE: The FMA in the PFD can also show a message that follows:

  • AP FAIL
  • AP/YD FAIL

Engage and disengage YD on FGCP
AVIONICS CB PANEL

E3 - YD AU
F7 - IOM/IOP/FGM1
F6 - IOM/IOP/FGM2
