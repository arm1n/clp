HOT MFD2

FIM TASK 31-61-01-810-815 (TR-FIM)

The ED shows this message when multi-function display 2 (MFD2) has overheated.

NOTE: The AVIONICS caution light will also come on.








NOTE:
  • Check cooling hose connections on display units.
  • Clean display cooling filters and air vents of display units, if necessary.
  • Might also happen in hot environment without proper cooling.
  • T/S according FIM 31-61-00 (display units, cooling fans, cooling air distribution)
