DU BAD CONF (ground only)


FAULT FIM TASK 31-61-01-810-806 (TR-FIM)

The set ED shows this message when a display unit (primary flight display 1 [PFD1], multifunction display 1 [MFD1], ED, MFD2, or PFD2) senses that it does not receive valid aircraft configuration data from the two aircraft configuration modules (ACM1 and ACM2), or that the data is different.

NOTE:
The DU BAD CONF message can come into view only after a display unit (DU) power-on self-test (POST).

NOTE:
The caution and warning panel (CAWP) AVIONICS caution light will also come on.









NOTE:

DU BAD CONFIG Message on ED appears after Power-up of Displays.

  • Turn PFD 1 and 2, MFD 1 and 2 and ED Off then On one at a time
      • NOTE: Wait 30 seconds for self-test to complete on each individual display before selecting the next display On.

  • Reset IOP 1 and IOP 2 circuit breakers on Avionics CB pane
      • IOP 1 (F7)
      IOP 2 (F6)

  • Check WOW Sensors

IF caution light remain on: - Maintenance action is required prior to flight.
