The ED shows the FANS FAIL message when one of the input/output modules (IOMs) senses that two or more cooling fans do not operate in the conditions that follow:

  • Aircraft is airborne
  • Aircraft is on the ground and the flight compartment temperature is more than 10Â°C.
      • NOTE: The AVIONICS caution light will also come on.


The FANS message shows on the CDS in the conditions that follow:

  • Defective right - hand zone temperature switch/sensor
  • Defective interface wiring between the cooling fan circuit breaker and the right - hand zone temperature switch/sensor
  • Defective interface wiring between the right - hand zone temperature switch/sensor and relay K5
  • Defective interface wiring between relay K5 and the fans.
