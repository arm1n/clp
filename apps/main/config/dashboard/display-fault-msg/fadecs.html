FADECS/DU FAIL ON ED

The ED shows this indication when it senses that the full authority digital electronic controllers (FADECS) output channels used for indication has malfunctioned for more than 10 seconds while the aircraft is less than 50 KIAS and the engines are not operating.



FADECS/DU FAIL ON MFD1/2

The MFD 1/2 shows this indication when it is set to show an engine display (ED) reversion and senses that the full authority digital electronic controllers (FADECS) output channels used for indication has malfunctioned for more than 10 seconds while the aircraft is less than 50 KIAS and the engines are not operating.
