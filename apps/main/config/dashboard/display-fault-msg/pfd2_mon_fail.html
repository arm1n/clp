The PFD2 MON FAIL message is usually out of view and comes into view when conditions are as follows:

  • The set ED senses that the Primary Flight Display 2 (PFD2) output bus has malfunctioned for more than 30 seconds
  • The PFD2 is set to show PFD2 indications. The Caution and Warning Panel (CAWP) AVIONICS caution light will also come on.
