Erroneous Elevator Trim Indication




(Indicator does not show full travel in either the nose up or nose down direction)

Pitch Trim Ind CB (F4 – Left Essential).. Reset.
NOTE: Elevator Trim indicator travels off-scale full ND (CB out) then returns on-scale between NU and ND index marks (CB in). Elevator Trim.. Operate elevator trim through full travel and check elevator trim indicator for full deflection nose up and nose down. NOTE: Full nose up or nose down position is defined by assuming the NU or ND index marks are shifted 1 arc width (needle width) toward the center of the indicator Elevator Trim.. Set as req’d

IF Elevator Trim indicator does not indicate full deflection in each direction Maintenance action is required prior to flight.
