IOP2 FAIL

FIM TASK 31-41-00-810-865 (TR-FIM)

The ED shows this message when it senses the Input/Output Processor 2 (IOP2) output has malfunctioned for more than 10 seconds.

NOTE: The AVIONICS caution light will also come on.








NOTE:

Reset IOP 1 and IOP 2

  • IOP 1 (F7) on avionics CB panel
  • IOP 2 (F6) on avionics CB panel
