“WTGS FAIL” (Message on ED)



WTGS FAIL on Associated with initial aircraft power-up:
  • Reset Simultaneously IOM/IOP/FGM 1 and 2 CB’s (F6 and F7- Avionics)
  • NOTE: Wait 30 seconds until self-test sequence is complete.

IF display remains in self-test: Maintenance action is required prior to flight.
