FADEC1/DU FAIL, ED (Advisory)

The ED shows this indication when it senses that a Full Authority Digital Electronic Control 1 (FADEC 1) output channel has malfunctioned for more than 10 seconds while the aircraft is less than 50 KIAS and the engines are not operating.



FADEC1/DU FAIL, MFD1 (Advisory)

The MFD1 shows this indication when it is set to show an engine display (ED) reversion and senses that a full authority digital electronic control 1 (FADEC 1) output channel has malfunctioned for more than 10 seconds while the aircraft is less than 50 KIAS and the engines are not operating.



FADEC1/DU FAIL, MFD2 (Advisory)

The MFD2 shows this indication when it is set to show an engine display (ED) reversion and senses that a full authority digital electronic control 1 (FADEC 1) output channel has malfunctioned for more than 10 seconds while the aircraft is less than 50 KIAS and the engines are not operating.
