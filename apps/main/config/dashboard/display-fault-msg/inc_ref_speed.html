INCR REF SPEED


On aircraft with Mod 4Q126089, the Stall Protection System (SPS) receives icing condition status data from the selection of the INCR position of the REF SPEEDS switch on the ICE PROTECTION panel.

When the propeller anti-ice switch is turned ON, the Stall Protection System (SPS) changes its calculations because of the reduced performance limits of the aircraft.

On aircraft with Mod 4Q126089, when the REF SPEEDS switch on the ICE PROTECTION panel is set to INCR position, the Stall Protection System (SPS) changes its calculations because of the reduced performance limits of the aircraft.

The Electronic Instrument System (EIS) shows an INCR REF SPEEDS message.
