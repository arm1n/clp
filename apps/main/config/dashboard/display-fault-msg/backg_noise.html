BACKGROUND NOISE IN HEADSETS




1. Check INT/RAD toggle switch on OBS audio control panel -> should be in neutral position. 2. Verify that no communication or navigation radio is causing the noise (turn all volumes off). 3. Verify that background noise is not caused by external power (power aircraft with battery power only, switch off external power). 4. Clean FWD and AFT ground crew connectors.

Reset all following circuit breakers

  • AVIONICS CB PANEL:
      • B8 PA AMP
      C11 COPLT SPKR
      C10 COPLT AUDIO
      C3 COPLT AUDIO
      C9 PLT SPKR
      D4 PLT AUDIO
      C4 OBS AUDIO

  • on PILOT´s CB PANEL
      • H2 PLT AUDIO

  • and on COPILOT´s CB panel.
      • M1 PA EMER PWR
