CUE


CUE message on PFD1 FIM TASK 27-33-00-810-830 (TR-FIM) CUE message on PFD2 FIM TASK 27-33-00-810-831 (TR-FIM)

This fault isolation procedure is for when the indicated air speed (IAS) indicator on primary flight display 1/2 (PFD1/2) shows a CUE indication. The IAS shows a CUE indication for the malfunction conditions that caused the

#1 STALL SYST FAIL caution light to come on as follows:

  • Stall protection module 1/2 (SPM1/2) is defective
  • Difference between angle of attack transducer 1/2 (AOA1/2) and AOA 2
  • AOA1 is defective or not heated
  • AOA2 is defective or not heated
  • The flap position indication unit (FPIU) left and right flap position inputs are defective


The IAS indicator also shows a CUE indication for the malfunction conditions that follow:

  • Air data unit 1 (ADU1) calibrated airspeed (CAS), true air speed (TAS), or pitot pressure input is defective
  • ADU2 CAS, TAS or pitot pressure input is defective
  • Attitude and heading reference system 1 (AHRS1) bank angle, pitch angle, body linear accelerations (z), body angular rates, or vertical speed input is defective
  • Full authority digital electronic controller 1 (FADEC1) (A and B channels) engine torque input is defective
  • FADEC2 (A and B channels) engine torque input is defective

NOTE:
The CUE flag is out of view when the aircraft is on the ground and the KIAS is less than 60 knots.








NOTE:

L and R AOA VANE HT CB`s (115V AC) - Check/In STICK PUSHER CB`s K1 and K6 Avionics - Reset simultaneously
NOTE:Wait 30 sec. for completion of self test.


IF caution light remain on: SPM 1 CB F2 Avionics - Reset SPM 2 CB F5 Avionics - Reset

NOTE:Wait 30 sec. for completion of self test.
