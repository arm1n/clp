AP PITCH TRIM FAIL


AP PITCH TRIM FAIL displayed on PFD FIM TASK 22-10-00-810-802 (TR-FIM)
The FMA shows this message for the conditions that follow:
  • The pitch actuator (APAU-P) torque values disagree
  • There is a malfunction of the left and right channels of the FCECU

Check AP disengage pushbuttons on control wheels for stuck condition, clean as necessary
