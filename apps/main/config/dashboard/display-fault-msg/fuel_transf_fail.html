FUEL FAILS TO TRANSFER



Associated with aircraft refuelled to full tanks on uneven ground
  • Taxi to level ground
  • Fuel Transfer... as req’d

IF fuel transfer is not possible:

  • Continue to consume fuel until the quantity indication in the low tank has decreased by the amount of the fuel imbalance.
  • Fuel Transfer... as req’d

IF fuel transfer is still not possible: Maintenance action is required prior to flight.
