OVERSPEED

Overspeed warning horn sounds during flight below Vmo

Inspection After Operation at More Than the Maximum Operating Speed AMM TASK 05-50-28-210-801 (TR-AMM) Inspection After Operation at More Than the Maximum Gear Operation Speed AMM TASK 05-50-31-210-801 (TR-AMM) Inspection After Operation at More than the Wing Flaps Down Speed AMM TASK 05-50-36-210-801 (TR-AMM)







NOTE:
  • If overspeed warning horn sounds during flight, although airspeed is below Vmo, than the warning is actually a wrongly triggered take-off warning.
  • Fault most likely related to defective WOW proximity sensor. Check all WOW sensors for proper operation (wiring and sensor!)
