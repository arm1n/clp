LDG GEAR INOP

FIM TASK 32-10-00-810-801 (TR-FIM)

The CAWP shows the LDG GEAR INOP on when the proximity sensor electronic unit (PSEU) landing gear control has failed.

NOTE: The CAWP can also show the LDG GEAR INOP on if the Landing Gear Inhibit Switch is not stowed correctly in its normal position or a loose conntact on the switch.



Functional test of the landing gear primary extension and retraction
