#2 RUD HYD

FIM TASK 27-20-00-810-805 (TR-FIM)

The CAWP #2 RUD HYD caution light comes on for the conditions that follow:
  • The right channel (CHNL 2) of the Flight Control Electronic Control Unit (FCECU) has sensed that it cannot control hinge moment limiting to the upper rudder Power Control Unit (PCU)

  • The upper rudder PCU in the No. 2 hydraulic system senses that its internal pressure is less than 350 psi (2413.16kPa)
    • NOTE: The #2 RUD HYD caution light will also come on when RUD 2 PUSH OFF annunciator switch on the center glareshield panel is set. This is not a malfunction condition.

  • The FCECU does not receive the correct pressure data from the No. 2 hydraulic system.
    • NOTE: The FCECU senses the pressure of the No. 2 hydraulic system after the engine is shutdown and then started again.
