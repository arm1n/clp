SPLR OUTBD

FIM TASK 27-60-00-810-805 (TR-FIM)

The CAWP SPLR OUTBD caution light comes on for the conditions that follow:
  • The flight control electronic control unit (FCECU) does not prevent outboard spoilers operation when the airspeed is more than 185 kt (342.62 km/h)
  • The FCECU prevents outboard spoilers operation when the airspeed is less than 150 kt (277.8 km/h)
  • The airspeed is invalid
