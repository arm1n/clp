EMER LTS DISARMED FIM TASK 33-50-00-810-802 (TR-FIM)
The CAWP EMER LTS DISARMED caution light comes on when the emergency power supplies are not armed. Thus, the EMER LTS DISARMED caution light should not come on when the EMER LIGHTS toggle switch is set to the ARM position.

NOTE: The EMER LTS DISARMED light will also come on for conditions that are not malfunctions as follows:
  • The EMER LIGHTS toggle switch is set to the OFF position
  • The EMER LIGHTS toggle switch is set to the ON position.


NOTE: The emergency lights can possibly come on and stay on or not come on when the EMER LTS DISARMED caution light is on.
