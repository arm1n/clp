ROLL SPLR INBD HYD

FIM TASK 27-60-00-810-803 (TR-FIM)

The CAWP ROLL SPOILER INBD HYD caution light comes on when the inboard spoiler hydraulic system pressure is low.

Spoiler Cable Sensor:
If the tension of the fuselage spoiler cables decreases to zero, the tension spring will pull the roller arm against a stop. In this position, the end of the roller arm will operate the switches. The normally−open switches are closed.
This gives a signal to the inboard and outboard unload valves to close. The spoiler PCU extend pressure is removed and the hold - down pressure moves all the spoilers to the retracted position. Also, the ROLL SPLR INBD HYD and ROLL SPLR OUTBD HYD caution lights will come on.
The latch spring will latch the roller arm and keep the spoiler sensor switches in the closed position until it is released by maintenance personnel on the ground.

Maintenance Practies:
Prior closing Fuselage Panel 121BL check that “SPOILER CABLE SENSOR” is not inadvertently activated! => If yes reset SENSOR according AMM TASK 27-61-00-710-805 (TR-AMM) prior next flight!

NOTE:

The spoiler cable sensor will automatically retract the spoilers if there is a failure of fuselage spoiler cable or by activation during maintenance and will  also activate both caution lights the ROLL SPLR INBD HYD and ROLL SPLR OUTBD HYD when No.1 and No. 2 hydraulic system is pressurized.


Release/Reset the “SPOILER CABLE SENSOR” roller arm from its latched condition as follows:
  • Lightly push the roller arm down to decrease the pressure of the roller arm against the spring.
  • Push the spring up and let the cable tension move the roller arm counterclockwise to the usual serviceable position.
  • Make sure that the roller is on the top spoiler cable.
  • Make sure that the grommet has a clearance of 0.10 to 0.30 in. (2.54 to 7.62 mm) around the bottom spoiler cable
  • Pressurize the No. 1 hydraulic system and 2 hydraulic system
  • In the flight compartment, make sure that the ROLL SPLR INBD HYD and the ROLL SPLR OUTBD HYD caution lights go out
