ELEVATOR ASYMETRY FIM TASK 27-30-00-810-801 (TR-FIM)
The CDS shows this message for the conditions that follow:

PRE SB84-27-14
  • The left and right elevator control surface positions are more than 6° different from each other when the airspeed is less than 185 knots
  • The left and right elevator control surface positions are more than 4° different from each other when the airspeed is more than 185 knots
  • The FCECU senses the elevator surface position linear variable differential transformer (LVDT) has malfunctioned
  • A failure of the surface position LVDT has been detected and the aircraft is on ground

POST SB84-27-14
  • When the flight control electronic control unit (FCECU) senses that the left and right elevator control surface positions are more than 6.0 ±1° different from each other when airspeeds is less than 215 knots for 2 seconds
  • The FCECU senses that the left and right elevator control surface positions are more than 4.5 ±1° different from each other when airspeed is more than 215 knots for 2 seconds
  • The FCECU senses the elevator surface position linear variable differential transformer (LVDT) has malfunctioned
  • A failure of the surface position LVDT has been detected and the aircraft is on ground

NOTE:
The caution and warning panel (CAWP) ELEVATOR ASYMMETRY caution light can also come on.

This procedure shall not be used if other caution lights associated with the pitch trim/pitch feel system or abnormalities of the elevator control system are observed.

Note: Wait 90 sec (self test in progress) ENG START - Start eingine #2 and un-feather propeller SPU - select SPU to "ON" ELEVATOR TRIM - set to "NEUTRAL" position ELEVATOR CONTROL - "hands-free"
(check LH & RH elevator indication on PFCS) ELEVATOR CONTROL - slowly move the columm through two full cycles and check elevator PFCS indication ( full deflection) PILOT`s PITCH TRIM SWITCH - 1/4 full scale ND and NU Repeat this three times
