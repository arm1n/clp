#2 ENG HYD PUMP


FIM TASK 29-30-00-810-805 (TR-FIM)

The CAWP #2 ENG HYD PUMP caution light comes on when the engine-driven pump (EDP) output pressure is less than 1800 psi.

NOTE: The #2 HYD ENG HYD PUMP caution light can also come on when the No. 1 PROP lever is set to FUEL OFF. This is not a malfunction condition.
Fluid Quantity Check of the No. 1 and No. 2 Hydraulic System Reservoirs
