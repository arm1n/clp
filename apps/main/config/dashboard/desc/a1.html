The CAWP PITCH TRIM caution light comes on when the flight control electronic control unit (FCECU) has sensed the conditions that follow:
  • Internal failure of the FCECU
  • Left pitch trim actuator malfunction
  • Right pitch trim actuator malfunction
  • ADC1 or ADC2 malfunction or mismatch
  • Pilot and co-pilot commands are different
  • Pitch trim "runaway" is sensed
The Elevator Feel and Trim unit provides artificial feel and trimming to counter the effects of longitudinal pitch control changes during all phases of flight. Trimming counters the effects of changes to the aircraft centre of gravity, airspeed and lift during flap extension or retraction.

The system includes two pitch trim actuators installed on top of the pitch feel and trim units supply elevator trim. The elevator trim actuator is controlled automatically by the autopilot or manually by the trim switches on the pilot's and copilot's control hand wheels.
