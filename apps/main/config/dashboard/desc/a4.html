ELEVATOR PRESS

FIM TASK 29-30-00-810-801 (TR-FIM)

The ELEVATOR PRESS caution light comes on, when all three hydraulic systems have high pressure.

NOTE: The ELEVATOR PRESS caution light can come on for conditions that are not malfunctions as follows:
  • No. 1 and No. 2 engines are operating and
  • The HYD #3 ISOL VALVE annunciator switch is set to OPEN
