ROLL SPLR INBD GND FIM TASK 27-60-00-810-801 (TR-FIM)
The CAWP ROLL SPLR INBD GND caution light comes on when the flight control electronic control unit (FCECU) senses a condition that follows:
  • The inboard spoiler panels do not extend after landing
  • The inboard spoiler panels extend during an event built-in test (EBIT)
  • One engine power lever angle (PLA) is set more than 47 degrees (12 degrees forward of the FLIGHT IDLE position) and the other is set less than 47 degrees

NOTE: The roll spoilers system has a glareshield panel SPOILERS FLIGHT TAXI toggle switch to give manual override of the spoilers lift dump function (spoilers extend) after the aircraft lands. After the aircraft lands and the spoilers extend, the SPOILERS FLIGHT TAXI toggle switch is set to the TAXI position to retract the spoilers.

Also, after the spoilers are retracted for more than 6.5 seconds, the FCECU does and EBIT. This makes sure that a spoiler lift-dump valve does not stay in the open position.

The roll spoilers system operates in the modes that follow:
  • Flight mode (the spoilers give roll control)
  • Ground mode (spoiler panels are fully extended)
  • Taxi mode (spoiler panels retract and operate like flight mode)

NOTE: The SPOILERS FLIGHT TAXI toggle switch has a magnetic coil that will hold the switch in the TAXI position when the two PLAs are set at less than 47 degrees. If PLA1 or PLA2 is set to more than 47 degrees, the switch automatically goes back to the FLIGHT position
