CABIN PRESS


FIM TASK 21-30-00-810-801 (TR-FIM)

The CAWP shows the CABIN PRESS warning when the cabin altitude is more than 10000 ft. (3048 m).

NOTE: This fault isolation procedure is not applicable when the take-off or landing elevation is more than 8000 ft. (2438.40 m).

NOTE: For take-off or landing elevations more than 8000 ft. (2438.40 m), the CABIN PRESS warning indication will come on in one of the two conditions that follow:
  • The aircraft take-off altitude plus 1000 ft. (304.80 m)
  • The landing altitude plus 1000 ft. (304.80 m)

If the CABIN PRESS FAULT LIGHT extinguished during the flight, to confirm system functionalityprior to the next flight, the following procedure must be conducted.



AFTER LANDING - AUTO/MAN/DUMP switch - Select DUMP After 5 seconds - AUTO/MAN/DUMP switch - Select AUTO.

If the FAULT light does not illuminate, the aircraft can continue further flight.


If the FAULT light illuminates continuously, maintenance action is required.
