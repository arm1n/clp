#2 HYD ISO VLV FIM TASK 29-10-00-810-804 (TR-FIM)
The #1(#2) HYD ISO VLV (caution) light indicates that the No. 1 and/or No. 2 isolation valve(s) has closed, and part of the hydraulic system has been isolated.

The isolation valve is operated by spring force. It is controlled by pilot pressure. The pilot pressure port is controlled by a reservoir level sensing valve, installed in the reservoirs. Two pressure switches are installed on each hydraulic system isolation valve.

These pressure switches monitor hydraulic pressure at the inlet and outlet ports of the isolation valve. When pressure is sensed at the inlet pressure switch, but not at the outlet, the No.1 or No.2 HYD ISO VLV caution light comes on.

No.1 hydraulic system isolation allows fluid flow to these components (only):

  • Flaps
  • PTU
  • Rudder
  • Elevators
No.2 hydraulic system isolation allows fluid flow to these components (only):

  • Rudder
  • Elevators
