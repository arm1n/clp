#2 HYD FLUID HOT


FIM TASK 29-30-00-810-806 (TR-FIM)

The CAWP #2 HYD FLUID HOT caution light comes on when the hydraulic reservoir fluid temperature is more than 225°F (107°C).

There are no related central diagnostic system messages.
