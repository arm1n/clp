FUSELAGE DOORS FIM TASK 52-70-00-810-801 (TR-FIM)
The FUSELAGE DOORS warning light comes on for conditions that follow:
  • A fuselage door is not closed
  • A fuselage door is not locked
  • A proximity sensor for the fuselage doors has malfunctioned

The FUSELAGE DOORS warning light comes on for conditions that follow:
  • FORWARD PAX
  • AFT PAX
  • AFT SERVICE
  • EMERG EXIT
  • FORWARD BAGGAGE
  • AFT BAGGAGE
