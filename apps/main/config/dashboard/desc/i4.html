#2 AC GEN FIM TASK 24-20-00-810-804 (TR-FIM)
The #2 AC GEN caution light in the CAWP comes on when the related AC Generator Control Unit (GCU) senses a condition that follows:
  • If a source fault is sensed, the AC generator line contactors K1 and K2 will not connect the AC GEN 1 to the 115 V AC left bus and the 115 V AC right bus.

The #2 AC GEN caution light will also come on for the conditions which are not malfunctions that follow:
  • The AC generator toggle switches are set to the OFF position
  • External AC power is energizing the L and R AC buses.

The Electrical Power Control Unit (EPCU) can show the related Continuous Built-In Test (CBIT) fault review codes:
  • 26, EPCU, #2 AC generator feeder (Status)
    • FIM TASK 24-21-00-810-802 (TR-FIM)

  • 28, EPCU, #2 AC Generator Control Unit (Status)
    • FIM TASK 24-21-06-810-802 (TR-FIM)
