#2 TANK FUEL LOW

FIM TASK 28-40-00-810-816 (TR-FIM)

The #2 TANK FUEL LOW caution light comes on in the conditions that follow:
  • There is less than 306 lbs (139 kgs) in the left fuel tank collector bay
  • The engine is operating
  • The parking brake is not set

NOTE: The #2 TANK FUEL LOW indication also comes on after the engines are not operated with low fuel quantity for a long period of time. The collector bay fuel level can be low because there is no fuel scavenging while the engines are not operating. This is not a malfunction condition.
