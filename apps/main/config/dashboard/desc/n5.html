The CAWP shows the WT ON WHEELS caution light for the conditions that follow:
  • The weight-on-wheels 1 (WOW1) and WOW2 calculation is different
  • One proximity sensor in each channel has malfunctioned
  • Nosewheel overtravel of more than 120 degrees
