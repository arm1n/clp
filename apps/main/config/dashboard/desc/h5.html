#1 ENG FADEC FIM TASK 77-31-00-810-828 (TR-FIM)
The CAWP #1 ENG FADEC caution light comes on when it is necessary to move the power lever slowly to keep the possibility of an engine surge to a minimum.

Also, it can be necessary to set the power levers to two different positions to keep symmetrical thrust between the two powerplants.

NOTE: These faults can cause power lever stagger or compressor stall (surging). The powerplant can make thrust and can be used to complete the flight, but special procedures are necessary to prevent power lever stagger or compressor stall.
