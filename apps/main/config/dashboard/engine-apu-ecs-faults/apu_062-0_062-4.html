LOW OIL PRESS SW FLT ( Status ) - (APU Fault Code 062-0 to 062-4)

FIM TASK 49-70-00-810-813 (TR-FIM)

The CDS shows a 62-4 LOW OIL PRESSURE SW FLT message when the auxiliary power unit (APU) full authority digital electronic controller (FADEC) senses a fault in the low oil pressure switch circuit during the watch mode.

The caution and warning panel (CAWP) APU and APU CONTROL panel FAIL caution lights can come on.

The list that follows shows the possible malfunctioned systems and/or line replaceable units (LRU):

  • APU FADEC
  • Low Oil Pressure Switch
