LEFT or Right AFT DUCT LEAK SWITCH - (ECS Fault Code 1802)

CH 1 (LH) FIM TASK 36-10-00-810-868 (TR-FIM) CH 2 (RH) FIM TASK 36-10-00-810-867 (TR-FIM)
Possible Causes:
  • The CDS shows this message when the left or right aft duct leak overtemperature switch circuit has high resistance .
  • The caution and warning panel (CAWP) #1 or #2 BLEED HOT caution light can also come on.
  • Faulty Wiring Harness between the Electronic Control Unit (ECU) and Left or Right Aft Duct Overtemperature Leak Switch
  • Faulty Left or Right Aft Duct Overtemperature Leak Switch
  • Faulty Environmental Control System (ECS) Electronic Control Unit (ECU)
The list that follows shows the possible malfunctioned systems and/or line replaceable units (LRU):
  • Environmental Control System Electronic Control Unit (ECU)
  • Aft Duct Leak Switch
