L HP BLD SW or R HP BLD SW - (ECS Fault Code 1301 or 1302)

CH 1 (LH) FIM TASK 36-10-00-810-804 (TR-FIM) CH 2 (RH) FIM TASK 36-10-00-810-801 (TR-FIM)
Possible Causes:
  • The CDS shows the L or R HP BLD SW message when the ARINC 429 discrete to the environmental control unit (ECU) indicates the HP pressure is low and you have all of the conditions that follow:
  • - The de-ice system is on
  • - The received P3 pressure indicates there is adequate bleed pressure (>92 psig)
  • The CDS shows the 1302 - L HP BLD SW message when the #1 Bleed switch is in the OFF position, the left engine’s HP pressure is below 70 psig, and the relay box is not commanding the HPSOV open.
  • The CDS shows the 1302 - R HP BLD SW message when the #2 Bleed switch is in the OFF position, the right engine’s HP pressure is below 70 psig, and the relay box is not commanding the HPSOV open.
  • Unserviceable wiring harness connector 2121-P29.
The list that follows shows the possible malfunctioned systems and/or line replaceable units (LRU):
  • Relay Junction Box 1, 2, 3
  • Ice and Rain Protection Panel
  • Bleed Stage Pressure Switch
