L BLD OVR TMP - (ECS Fault Code 7001)

CH 1 (LH) FIM TASK 36-10-00-810-852 (TR-FIM) CH 2 (RH) FIM TASK 36-10-00-810-864 (TR-FIM)
Possible Causes:
  • The CDS shows this message when a left or right bleed overtemperature condition is sensed. The overtemperature switch has sensed that the bleed air temperature is more than 349°C (660°F) .
  • The caution and warning panel (CAWP) #1 BLEED HOT or #2 BLEED HOTcaution light can also come on.
  • NOTE: Switch faults require WOW, bleeds, or packs set to OFF for at least five minutes to initiate this fault. Make sure to wait.
