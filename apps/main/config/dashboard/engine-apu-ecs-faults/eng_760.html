FIM TASK 73-20-00-810-802 (TR-FIM)

The fault isolation procedure that follows is to correct the full authority digital electronic control (FADEC) fault codes. The registered fault codes are 700 thru 703, 705, 707 thru 711, 715, 723, 739, 759, 760, 788, 791 thru 793, 806, 815, 923 thru 925, 927, 966, 974, 977 and/or 978.

Refer to FIM TASK 73-20-00-810-801 (TR-FIM) for a description of the type of fault codes and possible deferral opportunities.

  • Full Authority Digital Electronic Control (FADEC)
