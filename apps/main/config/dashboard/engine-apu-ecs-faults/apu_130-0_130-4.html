EGT T/C 1 AND 2 FAULT ( Status ) - (APU Fault Code 130-0 to 130-4)

FIM TASK 49-70-00-810-811 (TR-FIM)

The CDS shows a 130-4 EGT T/C 1 AND 2 FAULT message when the auxiliary power unit (APU) full authority digital electronic controller (FADEC) senses an open or short in the thermocouple 1 and thermocouple 2 circuits.

The CDS can also show the messages that follow:

126−5 EGT T/C 1 SHORT/127−5 EGT T/C 2 SHORT/128−5 EGT T/C 1 OPEN/129−5 EGT T/C 2 OPEN

The caution and warning panel (CAWP) APU and APU CONTROL panel FAIL caution lights can come on.

The list that follows shows the possible malfunctioned systems and/or line replaceable units (LRU):

  • APU FADEC
  • Thermocouple
