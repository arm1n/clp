HIGH OIL TEMP ( Status ) - (APU Fault Code 161-0 to 161-4)

FIM TASK 49-90-00-810-804 (TR-FIM)

The CDS shows a 161-4 HIGH OIL TEMP message when the auxiliary power unit (APU) full authority digital electronic controller (FADEC) senses a high oil temperature condition.

The caution and warning panel (CAWP) APU and APU CONTROL panel FAIL caution lights can come on.

The list that follows shows the possible malfunctioned systems and/or line replaceable units (LRU):

  • APU FADEC
  • High Oil Temperature Switch
  • Oil Filter Element
