PRESSURIZATION CONTROL

Functional Test of the Pressurization Control MANUAL AND AUTOMATIC AMM TASK 21-31-00-720-801 (TR-AMM)

Except for the pressure relief function, pressurization is controlled primarily by the electrically operated aft outflow valve. It is used for automatic and manual control, and can also be used to dump the pressurization. The forward safety valve is for emergency operation and for smoke removal from the flight deck. The aft safety valve and the forward safety valve have positive and negative pressure relief valves.

When cabin altitude is too high, the cabin pressure and the cabin indication module send the warning signal to the aircraft systems.
