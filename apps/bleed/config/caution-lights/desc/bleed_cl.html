BLEED - Cautions and Warnings

#1 BLEED HOT or #2 BLEED HOT caution light comes on for the conditions that follow:
  • Bleed overtempertaure
  • Bleed overpressure
  • Bleed duct leak

    The CAWP #1 BLEED HOT and #2 BLEED HOT caution lights come on for an aft bleed duct leak

         CABIN PACK HOT or FLT COMP PAK HOT caution light comes on when the cabin pack discharge temperature is more than 212C.
    The pack overtemperature shutdown monitor will stop the ACM pack in response to a pack overtemperature fault. This fault is monitored by the compressor outlet temperature sensor and the compressor overtemperature switch. This monitor will command the pack flow control valve to close.     FLT COMP DUCT HOT or CABIN DUCT HOT caution light comes on when when the temperature in either supply duct is greater than 190 °F (87.8 °C).
    This signal causes the ECU to turn on the related CABIN DUCT HOT or FLT COMP DUCT HOT caution light on the caution and warning panel.
    The ECU also shuts off the related air conditioning pack (by closing the turbine and ACM bypass shutoff valves).
