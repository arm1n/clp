FUEL FEED

Engine Fuel Feed

  • The engine fuel feed system supplies pressurized fuel to the aircraft engines.
  • Engine No.1 receives its fuel from tank No.1
  • Engine No.2 receives its fuel from tank No.2

APU Fuel Feed

  • The APU fuel feed system supplies fuel to the APU. Fuel for the APU is supplied by gravity feed from the No.1 fuel tank.
