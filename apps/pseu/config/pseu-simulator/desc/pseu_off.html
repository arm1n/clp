PROXIMITY SENSOR SYSTEM

The PSS uses 31 proximity sensors and 5 discrete inputs to give the position of the landing gear, the gear doors and the fuselage doors. The outputs of the sensors are processed by the PSEU. The PSEU gives 104 identified discrete outputs to control the landing gear. They also show the position of the landing gear components, aircraft on ground, gear doors and fuselage doors.

The Proximity Sensor System (PSS) is a part of the landing gear system. The system gives:

  • Landing Gear Control (LGC)
  • Landing Gear status Indication (LGI)
  • Alternate Downlock Indication (ADI)
  • Weight−On−Wheels indication (WOW1 and WOW2)
  • Fuselage Door Status Indication (FDSI) to the flight compartment and other aircraft systems
