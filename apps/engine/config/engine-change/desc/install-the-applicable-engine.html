Installation of the Engine in the Nacelle (General)


Make sure that the aircraft is in the same configuration as in the removal task.
General Visual Inspection Exposed Area of The Nacelle Forward Frame Assembly, Includes Lower Cowl Attachment Lugs and Strut Attachment Fittings, LH and RH AMM TASK 54-10-01-210-801 - (TR-AMM)


General Visual Inspection of the Exposed Area of the Nacelle Mid-Frame Assembly (Includes the Lower Cowl Attachment Lugs and Strut Attachment Fittings) AMM TASK 54-10-01-210-802 - (TR-AMM)
NOTE:

MAKE SURE THAT THE INTAKE AIR PASSAGE IS CLEAR OF UNWANTED OBJECTS BEFORE INSTALLATION. IF YOU DO NOT DO THIS, IT CAN CAUSE DAMAGE TO THE ENGINE.

Prepare to install the engine in the nacelle as follows

  • Put the engine and the stand in position under the nacelle
      • NOTE: It is possible to install the engine in the nacelle from the shipping container. In this case, put the engine and the shipping container under the nacelle, approximately 12 in. (305 mm) forward of the fully installed position.


  • Attach the engine hoist to the forward and aft lifting points on the engine.
      • NOTE: Install the rear lifting strut (4) so that the cut-out faces aft.


  • CAUTION:
      • DO NOT LIFT THE ENG WHEN IT IS ATTACHED TO THE BASE. YOU CAN CAUSE DAMAGE TO THE ENGINE.
      DO NOT LIFT THE ENG WHEN IT IS ATTACHED TO THE STAND. YOU CAN CAUSE DAMAGE TO THE ENGINE.


  • With the hoist, lift the engine from the stand or lift the engine from the container
      • AMM TASK 71-00-00-000-802 - (TR-AMM)


  • Remove all the shipping closures from the engine.


  • Lift the engine to the correct height, then move it aft until the engine mount locations are aligned with the nacelle mount locations.


  • Start to install the Install the isolators
