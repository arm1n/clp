Filling of the Engine Oil System



Experience advise:

For a newly installed engine or if the oil tank was drained, fill the engine oil system prior you do an engine run. It is recommended to use the sight glass for checking the oil level.

To ensure that enough oil is in the oil tank it is necessary to do a dry motor run.


NOTE:
Normal filling quantity with empty oil tank 35 liters
