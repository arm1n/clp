AUXILIARY POWER UNIT (APU), GENERAL

The APS 1000−C12 Auxiliary Power Unit (APU) is a turbine−powered system used to give auxiliary electrical power and pneumatic power.

The APU engine and its associated components are installed in the aft section of the tail cone. Consisting of a gas turbine engine driving a starter/generator, the APU gives bleed air to the air conditioning pack and 28 Vdc to the left main feeder bus. The APU is installed for ground use only and cannot be operated in flight. The APU has a control system, an air induction system, an exhaust system, a gearbox, accessories, an electrical generator.
