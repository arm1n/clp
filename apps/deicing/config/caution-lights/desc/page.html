CAUTION LIGHTS

Failure of "both" Ice Detection Probes (IDPs) is shown by the ICE DETECT FAIL caution light on the Caution and Warning Panel coming on. Failure of "one" Ice Detection Probe (IDP) is reported to the Timer Monitor Unit (TMU). The TMU stores the failure as a maintenance message that will be displayed on the Engine Systems Integrated Display (ESID) when the Centralized Diagnostic System (CDS) is interrogated. The displayed message is “LEFT IDP FAIL” or “RIGHT IDP FAIL” for the related left or right Ice Detection Probe (IDP) failure.

If either AIC fails, the WSHLD CTRL caution light will come on. Failure messages are recorded in the Timer and Monitor Unit (TMU) for maintenance purposes. Two maintenance messages related to the failures are RIGHT AIC FAILRIGHT AIC FAIL and LEFT AIC FAIL.

An overheat condition of the pilot’s Side Window is shown by the SIDE WDO HOT caution light coming on.

An overheat condition of the pilot’s or copilot’s windshield is shown by the WSHLD HOT caution light coming on.

When the probe heater is selected on and the heater electrical current is out of specified limits, or if the heating is not activated, the Timer Monitor Unit (TMU) senses a defective heater and the respective PITOT HEAT 1, PITOT HEAT 2, or STBY PITOT caution light on the Caution and Warning Panel comes on. Fault codes are stored in the Timer Monitor Unit (TMU) for maintenance purposes.

The maintenance messages associated with the fault codes are:
  • PITOT 1 FAIL
  • PITOT 2 FAIL
  • PITOT STBY FAIL
 The CAWP DEICE TIMER caution light comes on when the deice timer monitor unit has failed.(Airframe Pneumatic Deicing Systems)
The central diagnostic system (CDS) can show the related messages that follow:
  • MONITOR 1 FAIL
  • MONITOR 2 FAIL
  • TIMER/MONITOR 3 FAIL
  • TIMER INPUT FAIL
The CAWP DEICE PRESS caution light comes on for the conditions that follow:
  • One or more boot inflation or dwell pressure is not sensed by the timer and monitor unit (TMU) in four seconds
  • Left or right deice manifold pressure less than 12.5 psig is sensed by the left or right low pressure warning switch.

Time Monitor Control Unit (TMCU) has malfunctioned or it has sensed a malfunction in the prop deice system.

If both heating elements in the engine adapter heater fail, the related ENG ADPT HEAT 1 or ENG ADPT HEAT 2 caution light on theCaution and Warning panel to comes on.
