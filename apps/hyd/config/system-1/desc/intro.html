HYDRAULIC SYSTEM #1 Introduction

The Dash 8 Q400 has four hydraulic systems, three main systems, and an auxiliary system.

The No. 1 and No. 2 independent main systems provide power to operate the:
  • Flight Controls
  • Landing Gear
  • Nose Wheel Steering
  • Brake Systems

The No. 3 main hydraulic system supplies pressure to the left and right elevators if the No. 1 and / or No. 2 hydraulic system(s) fail.

The auxiliary hand operated hydraulic system provides power to the emergency main landing gear extension system.
